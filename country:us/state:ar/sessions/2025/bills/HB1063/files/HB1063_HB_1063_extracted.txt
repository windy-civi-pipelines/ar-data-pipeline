24 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:

26 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 20-3-103(4), concerning the definition of
27 "disability certification" under the Achieving a Better Life Experience
28 Program Act, is amended to read as follows:
29 (4) “Disability certification” means, with respect to an
30 individual, a certification to the satisfaction of the United States
31 Secretary of the Treasury by the individual or the parent or guardian of the
32 individual that:
33 (A) Certifies that:
34 (i)(a)(1) The individual has a medically
35 determinable physical or mental impairment that:
36 (A) Results in marked and severe

14 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 20-3-103(5), concerning the definition of

18 year:
19 (A) Is entitled to benefits based on blindness or

22 that occurred before the date on which the individual attained twenty-six

24 (B) Has a disability certification filed with the United
25 States Secretary of the Treasury for the taxable year;
26
27 SECTION 3. DO NOT CODIFY. Effective date.
