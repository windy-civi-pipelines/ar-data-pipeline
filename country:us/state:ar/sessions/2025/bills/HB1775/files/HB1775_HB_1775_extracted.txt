19 TO PROHIBIT THE SEPARATE VALUATION AND
20 ASSESSMENT OF AN ACCESSORY DWELLING UNIT
21 FOR PURPOSES OF PROPERTY TAXES; AND TO
22 AMEND THE METHOD OF VALUATION FOR
23 CERTAIN RESIDENTIAL PROPERTY UNDER
24 ARKANSAS CONSTITUTION, ARTICLE 16, § 5.
25
26 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
27
28 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 26-26-1202(a)(1), concerning the valuation
29 procedures for property for purposes of property taxes, is amended to read as
30 follows:
31 (a)(1)(A) Each separate parcel of real property shall be valued at its
32 true market value in money, excluding the value of crops growing thereon.
33 (B)(i) Residential property used as the principal place of
34 residence of the owner shall not be subdivided into multiple parcels to
35 create a separate parcel for an accessory dwelling unit.
36 (ii) Residential property used as the principal
