16 TO REQUIRE MEDICAID COVERAGE FOR
17 POSTPARTUM MOTHERS FOR ONE YEAR AFTER
18 GIVING BIRTH.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
21
22 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 20, Chapter 77, Subchapter 1, is
23 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
24 20-77-154. Extension of one year for postpartum coverage.
25 (a) The Department of Human Services shall:
26 (1) Extend postpartum Medicaid coverage for mothers to one (1)
27 year in duration; and
28 (2) Take necessary action, including without limitation
29 submitting and applying for a state plan amendment or waiver, to obtain
30 federal approval to adopt one-year postpartum Medicaid coverage for mothers
31 who have recently given birth.
32 (b) The department shall promulgate rules to administer this section.
