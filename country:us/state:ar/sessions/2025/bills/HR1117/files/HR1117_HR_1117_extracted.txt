15 TO RECOGNIZE FORMER REPRESENTATIVE BEN
16 MCGEE FOR HIS SERVICE TO HIS COMMUNITY
17 AND TO THE STATE OF ARKANSAS.
18
19 WHEREAS, Ben McGee of Marion is a former state representative who
20 represented the thirty-fifth district in Arkansas, which includes portions of
21 Crittenden County and Cross County in the Arkansas Delta, from 1988 until
22 1998; and
23
24 WHEREAS, former Representative McGee was the first African American
25 since Reconstruction to represent this district, which is today represented
26 by Representative Jessie McGruder; and
27
28 WHEREAS, former Representative McGee was also the first African
29 American elected to the school board of the Marion School District; and
30
31 WHEREAS, former Representative McGee was also the first African
32 American Chair of the Board of Trustees of Arkansas State University; and
33
34 WHEREAS, former Representative McGee showed great courage as a pioneer
35 during the Civil Rights Movement; and
36

2 sons and two (2) daughters, three (3) of whom are doctors,

4 NOW THEREFORE,
5 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
6 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
7
8 THAT the House of Representatives recognize former Representative Ben
9 McGee for his service to his community and to the State of Arkansas.
10
11 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
12 presented to Ben McGee by the Chief Clerk of the House of Representatives.
