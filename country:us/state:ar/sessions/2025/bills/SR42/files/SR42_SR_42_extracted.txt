16 TO RECOGNIZE THE CITY OF CABOT AS
17 RECIPIENT IN 2024 OF A TRENDSETTER CITY
18 AWARD FROM ARKANSAS BUSINESS AND AN
19 HONORABLE MENTION IN THE ARKANSAS
20 BUSINESS TOURISM DEVELOPMENT AND
21 CREATIVE CULTURE CATEGORY.
22
23 WHEREAS, the City of Cabot was recognized by Arkansas Business as a
24 2024 Trendsetter City in the Technology and Security category for its
25 broadband initiative; and
26
27 WHEREAS, Arkansas Business Trendsetter City status is a status which
28 recognizes cities and towns throughout the State of Arkansas whose innovative
29 initiatives for community development and economic growth inspire change and
30 make a difference in Arkansas, improving local quality of life and
31 encouraging sustainable economic development; and
32
33 WHEREAS, the City of Cabot partnered with Connect2First to develop a
34 comprehensive fiber optic network that covers every resident and business in
35 the city limits, a twenty million dollar ($20,000,000) bond project that has
36 boosted economic development, improved health care and educational access,

1 and made the City of Cabot one of Arkansas's first "multi-gig" cities; and
2
3 WHEREAS, the City of Cabot also received an honorable mention in the
4 Arkansas Business Tourism Development and Creative Culture category for
5 Gametime at the Grounds, a regional indoor mega sports complex that is the
6 first and only one of its kind in the State of Arkansas; and
7
8 WHEREAS, Gametime at the Grounds in the City of Cabot is a one hundred
9 twenty-eight thousand square foot (128,000 sq. ft.) sports facility boasting
10 nine (9) full-sized high school regulation basketball courts that transition
11 to seventeen (17) full-sized volleyball courts or twenty-seven (27)
12 pickleball courts, and the facility also features a large indoor turf area
13 designed for regulation indoor soccer and softball and baseball batting
14 practice; and
15
16 WHEREAS, Gametime at the Grounds is a project expected to generate
17 significant revenue for local businesses and strengthen the reputation of the
18 City of Cabot as a sports hub; and
19
20 WHEREAS, the City of Cabot's designation as a Trendsetter City by
21 Arkansas Business reflects the city's role in advancing regional prosperity
22 with creative strategies and community collaboration and is a powerful
23 testament to the city's strong position for future growth in business and
24 development; and
25
26 WHEREAS, the City of Cabot was featured in the December 16, 2024,
27 edition of Arkansas Business magazine, and was further celebrated at the
28 Arkansas Municipal League's annual Winter Meeting in January 2025; and
29
30 WHEREAS, the City of Cabot's commitment to visionary leadership,
31 resident engagement, and successful growth strategies places it among
32 Arkansas's most pioneering communities, and the city strategically balances
33 the revitalization of its historic downtown with innovation of technology for
34 the future,
35
36 NOW THEREFORE,
2 03/11/2025 8:16:50 AM ADF092
SR42
1 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE
2 STATE OF ARKANSAS:
3
4 THAT the Senate recognize the City of Cabot as recipient in 2024 of a
5 Trendsetter City award from Arkansas Business.
6
7 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
8 provided to the leaders of the City of Cabot by the Secretary of the Senate.
