26 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code ยง 6-18-114(b)(5), concerning requirements a
27 public school that operates primarily as a virtual school and that
28 administers a statewide student assessment to a student in a virtual setting
29 must meet, is amended to read as follows:
30 (5)(A) An adult approved in accordance with terms established by

33 through grade eight (K-8) in a virtual setting, including without limitation
34 the student's parent, legal guardian, or person standing in loco parentis to
35 the student, is physically present with the student for the duration of the
36 administration of the statewide student assessment.
