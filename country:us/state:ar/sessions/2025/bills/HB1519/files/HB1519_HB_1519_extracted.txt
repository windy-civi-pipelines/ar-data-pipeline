27 (a) The General Assembly finds that:
28 (1) Shared services allow state agencies to streamline

33 information technology, to enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and minimize
34 waste while complying with state and federal law; and
35 (3) Shared services allow state agencies to benefit from
36 standardized services, encourages collaboration, interagency cooperation, and
