14 TO RECOGNIZE THE BENTON HIGH SCHOOL
15 PANTHERS BOYS' BASKETBALL TEAM AS THE
16 CLASS 5A STATE CHAMPIONS.
17
18 WHEREAS, the Benton High School Panthers boys' basketball team won the
19 Class 5A state championship held at Bank OZK Arena in Hot Springs, Arkansas,
20 on March 14, 2025; and
21
22 WHEREAS, this was the Panthers' first state championship win in
23 basketball; and
24
25 WHEREAS, the Panthers beat rival Maumelle by a score of fifty-eight
26 (58) to fifty-seven (57) points; and
27
28 WHEREAS, a successful layup with fewer than four (4) seconds remaining
29 by team member Terrion Burgess clinched the state championship victory for
30 the Panthers; and
31
32 WHEREAS, the Panthers' overall season record was thirty-one wins and
33 three losses (31-3); and
34
35 WHEREAS, the Panthers are also back-to-back Class 5A South Conference
36 champions; and

2 WHEREAS, the Panthers' conference record for the last two (2) years was
3 thirty-two wins and zero losses (32-0); and
4
5 WHEREAS, during the 2024-2025 conference season, the Panthers outscored
6 their opponents by one thousand one hundred ninety (1,190) points to six
7 hundred thirty (630) points, and averaged a thirty-five-point win in each
8 game; and
9
10 WHEREAS, the Panthers set school records for most wins with twenty-nine
11 (29) wins during the 2023-2024 season and thirty (30) wins during the 2024-
12 2025 season; and
13
14 WHEREAS, the 2024-2025 team members for the Panthers are Peyton
15 Bolding, Terrion Burgess, Carson Douglas, Pratt Fulks, A.D. Gray, Michael
16 Hendrix, Jack Hope, Chris Johnson, Xavier Jones, Caleb Knight, Jeremiah Lea,
17 Harrison Pickett, Javarious Russell, Elem Shelby, Braden Smith, Anthony
18 Spratt, Jr., and D.J. Trotter, and team managers are Henk Geimer and Will
19 Sams; and
20
21 WHEREAS, team member Terrion Burgess earned All-State Honors and was
22 named the Most Valuable Player of the tournament; and
23
24 WHEREAS, team member Harrison Pickett earned All-State Honors and was
25 named to the All-State Tournament Team, and he signed a Division 1
26 scholarship with the University of Arkansas at Pine Bluff, making him one (1)
27 of only a few students in school history to have signed with a Division 1
28 university; and
29

31 is the first freshman in school history to do so; and
32
33 WHEREAS, team member Elem Shelby earned All-Conference Honors and was
34 named to the All-State Tournament Team; and
35

1 all earned All-Conference Honors; and
2
3 WHEREAS, the Panthers were led by Head Coach Dexter Hendrix and
4 Assistant Coaches Tyler Gattin, Terry Burgess, Kendrick Smith, and Luke
5 Flowers; and
6
7 WHEREAS, Head Coach Dexter Hendrix was honored as the Class 5A South
8 Conference Coach of the Year during both the 2023-2024 and 2024-2025 seasons;
9 and
10
11 WHEREAS, Head Coach Dexter Hendrix said of the win and the season,
12 "This season was truly remarkable. This team set a new school record for the
13 most wins in a single season, achieving an impressive thirty-one victories.
14 They also secured back-to-back conference championships, extending their
15 conference winning streak to thirty-two games. Their resilience and
16 determination throughout the year were exceptional, and I couldn't be prouder
17 of the way they concluded the season. This team will forever be etched in the
18 history of Benton High School as the first to win a state championship",
19
20 NOW THEREFORE,
21 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
22 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
23
24 THAT the House of Representatives recognize the Benton High School
25 Panthers boys' basketball team as the Class 5A state champions.
26
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
28 presented to Head Coach Dexter Hendrix by the Chief Clerk of the House of
29 Representatives.
