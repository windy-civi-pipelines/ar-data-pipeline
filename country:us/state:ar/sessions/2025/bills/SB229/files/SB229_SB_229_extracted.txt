19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
20
21 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 23, Chapter 45, Subchapter 1, is
22 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
23 23-45-107. Department of Financial Services — Creation.
24 (a) There is created within the Department of Commerce the Department
25 of Financial Services.
26 (b) The Department of Financial Services shall consist of:
27 (1) The State Bank Department; and
28 (2) State Securities Department.
29 (c) The Bank Commissioner shall be the Director of the Department of
30 Financial Services.
31
32 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 25-43-302(a)(21), concerning the state
33 entities within the Department of Commerce, is amended to read as follows:
34 (21) The State Bank Department, created under § 23-46-201,
35 within the Department of Financial Services;
