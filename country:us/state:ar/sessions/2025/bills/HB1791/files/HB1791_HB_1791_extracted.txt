24 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:

26 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 1, Chapter 1, Subchapter 1, is amended
27 to add an additional section to read as follows:
28 1-1-104. Certain international organizations — Jurisdiction and
29 enforcement or implementation authority.
30 (a) The World Health Organization and the United Nations do not have
31 jurisdiction or power within this state.
32 (b) A requirement or mandate issued by the World Health Organization
33 or the United Nations shall not be:
34 (1) Enforced or implemented by this state or a political
35 subdivision of this state, including without limitation a state agency,
36 county, city, town, precinct, water district, school district, school
