16
17 WHEREAS, financial literacy is a fundamental life skill that empowers
18 individuals to make informed and responsible financial decisions, ensuring a
19 stable and secure future for themselves and their families; and
20
21 WHEREAS, Teach Children to Save Day, observed on April 24, 2025, is a
22 nationwide initiative that encourages parents, teachers, and caregivers to
23 educate young students on the importance of saving money and making smart
24 financial choices; and
25
26 WHEREAS, research has shown that early financial education helps
27 children develop critical skills such as budgeting, goal-setting, self-
28 discipline, and delayed gratification, which are essential for long-term
29 financial success; and
30
31 WHEREAS, many adults struggle with managing their finances due to a
32 lack of early education on the topic, making it imperative that we instill
33 strong financial habits in our children from an early age to foster a
34 generation of financially responsible and independent citizens; and
35
36 WHEREAS, through collaboration with local businesses, schools, and
