Subtitle
15
16 TO PERMANENTLY BAN FROM COACHING A COACH
17 OR AN ATHLETICS PERSONNEL MEMBER WHO
18 RECRUITS A STUDENT.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
21
22 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 6, Chapter 17, Subchapter 1, is amended
23 to add an additional section to read as follows:
24 6-17-125. Recruitment — Prohibitions — Definitions.
25 (a) As used in this section:
26 (1) "Athletics personnel" means athletic directors and other
27 individuals actively involved in organizing, training, or coaching athletic
28 events for student athletes;
29 (2) "Recruit" means the use of undue influence or special
30 inducement by a coach or an athletics personnel member employed by a school
31 that is a member of the Arkansas Activities Association in an attempt to
32 encourage, induce, pressure, urge, or entice a prospective student of any age
33 to transfer to the school or retain a student at the school for the purpose
34 of participating in extracurricular activities; and
35 (3) "School" means a public school district or an open-
36 enrollment public charter school.
