10 THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY; TO STATE THE
11 FUNCTIONS OF THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES ALLOWED TO
12 CONTINUE FOLLOWING ADJOURNMENT UNTIL THE CLOSE OF
13 BUSINESS EACH DAY; AND FOR OTHER PURPOSES.
14
15
Subtitle
16
17 TO AMEND THE RULES OF THE HOUSE OF
18 REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH
19 GENERAL ASSEMBLY.
20
21 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
22 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
23
24 SECTION 1. Subsection 33.(m) of the Rules of the House of
25 Representatives of the Ninety-Fifth General Assembly, concerning the daily
26 order of business, is amended to read as follows:
27 33.(m)(1) Adjournment.
28 (2) Unless modified by the motion to adjourn, the following
29 items are permitted to continue until the close of business on the day the
30 adjournment motion is made:
31 (A) Announcements;
32 (B) Reading of bills;
33 (C) Transferring bills;
34 (D) A member amending his or her own bill;
35 (E) A member withdrawing his or her own bill;
36 (F) Placing the calendars on members' desks;
