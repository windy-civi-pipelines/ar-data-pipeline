20 1-5-101. Official holidays.
21 (a) The following days are declared to be the sole official holidays

25 January;

HB1162
1 (11)(12) An employee's birthday — an employee is granted one (1)
2 holiday to observe his or her birthday.
3 (b) A holiday falling on a Saturday will be observed on the preceding
4 Friday. A holiday falling on a Sunday will be observed on the succeeding
5 Monday.
6 (c) It is the specific intent of the General Assembly that all state
7 employees shall be entitled to eleven (11) twelve (12) paid holidays per
8 year. The Office of Personnel Management shall promulgate rules and
9 regulations to assure this legislative intent.
