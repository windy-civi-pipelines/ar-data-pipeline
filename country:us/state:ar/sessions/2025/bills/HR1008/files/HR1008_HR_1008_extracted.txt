22 WHEREAS, Michael Todd of Hot Springs, Arkansas, was born on June 12,
23 1973; and
24
25 WHEREAS, Michael Todd competed in his first armwrestling tournament in
26 1990 at the Saline County Fair at seventeen (17) years of age; and
27
28 WHEREAS, Michael Todd at nineteen (19) years of age competed in his
29 first professional armwrestling championship tournament; and
30
31 WHEREAS, Michael Todd has won twenty-three (23) World Armwrestling
32 titles; and
33
34 WHEREAS, Michael Todd has won thirty-six (36) National Armwrestling
35 titles; and

1 WHEREAS, Michael Todd continues to this day to compete and win
2 championships at the highest levels of professional armwrestling; and
3
4 WHEREAS, Michael Todd has solidified his name as one of the greatest
5 armwrestlers in the history of the sport; and
6
7 WHEREAS, Michael Todd’s accomplishments and commitment to excellence
8 serve as an inspiration to all Arkansans,
9
10 NOW THEREFORE,
11 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
12 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
13
14 THAT the House of Representatives recognize “Monster” Michael Todd for
15 his inspiring athletic achievements in the sport of armwrestling and for
16 representing the State of Arkansas honorably to the world.
17
18 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
19 provided to “Monster” Michael Todd by the Chief Clerk of the House of
20 Representatives.
