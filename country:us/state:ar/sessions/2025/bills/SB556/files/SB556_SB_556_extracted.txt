9 AN ACT TO PROTECT PUBLIC DRINKING WATER SOURCES; TO

15 TO PROTECT PUBLIC DRINKING WATER
16 SOURCES; AND TO PROHIBIT SURFACE
17 DISCHARGE OF WASTEWATER INTO A
18 WATERSHED.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
21
22 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 8, Chapter 3, Subchapter 1 is amended
23 to add an additional section to read as follows:
24 8-3-104. Watershed discharge prohibition.
25 (a) A person or entity shall not discharge via surface discharge
26 wastewater into a watershed of any source of public drinking water at any
27 point upstream of where water is taken from the stream, river, lake, or other
28 body of water by a public water system.
29 (b) This section does not apply to discharge permitted under the
30 National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System stormwater discharge program
31 or the state stormwater discharge program.
32 (c) The Division of Environmental Quality shall enforce this section.
