21 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code ยง 20-13-108(b), concerning the triage, treat,
22 and transport to alternative destination by an ambulance service operator, is
23 amended to read as follows:
24 (b) An Except as provided under subsection (c) of this section, an
25 ambulance service's operators may triage and transport a patient to an
26 alternative destination in this state or treat in place if the ambulance
27 service is coordinating the care of the patient through telemedicine with a
28 physician for a medical-based complaint or with a behavioral health
29 specialist for a behavioral-based complaint.
30
31 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code ยง 20-13-108, concerning the triage, treat,
32 and transport to alternative destination by ambulance service operators, is

34 (c)(1) The Emergency Medical Services Advisory Council shall develop a
35 set of protocols for 9-1-1 emergency medical situations that are exempt from
36 the telemedicine requirement under subsection (b) of this section by rule.
