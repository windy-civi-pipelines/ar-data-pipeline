16 TO RECOGNIZE NATIONAL FFA WEEK.
17
18 WHEREAS, the National FFA Organization and agricultural education
19 provide a strong foundation for the youth of America and the future of the
20 food, fiber, and natural resources system; and
21
22 WHEREAS, the Arkansas FFA Association officers and the National FFA
23 Organization officers serve as role models who inspire and encourage members
24 to achieve premier leadership, personal growth, and career success; and
25
26 WHEREAS, the Arkansas FFA Association currently has 17,503 members,
27 with 217 local chapters and 335 advisors; and
28
29 WHEREAS, agricultural education and the FFA ensure a steady supply of
30 young professionals to meet the growing demands in the science, business, and
31 technology of agriculture; and
32
33 WHEREAS, the FFA motto, "Learning to Do, Doing to Learn, Earning to
34 Live, Living to Serve", gives direction and purpose to these students who
35 actively pursue excellence in agricultural education; and
36

1 WHEREAS, the FFA promotes citizenship, volunteerism, patriotism, and
2 cooperation; and
3
4 WHEREAS, National FFA Week is celebrated February 15 through February
5 22, 2025,
6
7 NOW THEREFORE,
8 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
9 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
10
11 THAT the House of Representatives recognize the importance and positive
12 work of the National FFA Organization, commend the Arkansas FFA Association
13 officers and the National FFA Organization officers for their leadership, and
14 recognize February 15 through February 22, 2025, as National FFA Week.
15
16 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption of this resolution, an appropriate
17 copy be provided to the Arkansas FFA Association and the National FFA
18 Organization by the Chief Clerk of the House of Representatives.
