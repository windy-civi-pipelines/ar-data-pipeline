15 TO AMEND THE LAW CONCERNING ENERGY; AND
16 TO REGULATE NONEXPORT FACILITIES.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
19
20 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 23, Chapter 18, Subchapter 1, is
21 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
22 23-18-111. Nonexport facility — Definition — Rules.
23 (a) As used in this section, "nonexport facility" means a facility for
24 the production of electrical energy to meet all or part of a customer's need
25 for electrical energy within a single electric utility's allocated service
26 territory that does not intentionally feed electricity back to the electric
27 utility.
28 (b)(1) On or before March 1, 2026, following notice, opportunity for
29 public comment, and a hearing, the Arkansas Public Service Commission shall
30 promulgate appropriate rules for a nonexport facility.
31 (2) The rules promulgated under subdivision (b)(1) of this
32 section shall include without limitation:
33 (A) Standards for the safe, reliable, and acceptable
34 operation of a nonexport facility, including:
35 (i) Thermal, service voltage, power quality, or
36 network limits associated with inadvertent export events; and

7 (B) An expedited review process for:
8 (i) Evaluating and approving acceptable export

10 (ii) Interconnection.
11
12 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 23-18-603(9), concerning the definition of
13 "net-metering facility" used under the Arkansas Cost-Shifting Prevention Act
14 of 2023, is amended to add an additional subdivision to read as follows:
15 (G) Does not include a nonexport facility as defined in §
16 23-18-111.
