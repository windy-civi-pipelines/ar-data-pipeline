17 TO AUTHORIZE CERTIFIED NURSE AIDES OR

22
23 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
24
25 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 20, Chapter 77, Subchapter 1, is
26 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
27 20-77-154. Certified nurse aides providing in-home services.
28 (a) The Arkansas Medicaid Program shall authorize certified nurse
29 aides or certified nursing assistants who provide in-home services to
30 beneficiaries to perform additional care and duties if:
31 (1) The certified nurse aide or certified nursing assistant has
32 been appropriately trained on the skill that is being performed or assisted
33 with; and
34 (2) The beneficiary or the representative of the beneficiary
35 consents to the performance of the care and duties by the certified nurse
36 aide or certified nursing assistant.
