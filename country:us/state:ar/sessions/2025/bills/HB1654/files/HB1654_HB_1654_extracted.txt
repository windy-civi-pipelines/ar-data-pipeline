28 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 27, Chapter 24, Subchapter 14, is
29 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
30 27-24-1433. Make-A-Wish Foundation of the Mid South.
31 (a) The Secretary of the Department of Finance and Administration
32 shall create and issue a Make-A-Wish Foundation of the Mid South special
33 license plate in the manner and subject to the conditions provided for under
34 this subchapter.
35 (b) The Make-A-Wish Foundation of the Mid South special license plate
36 shall be:

1 (1) Designed by the Department of Finance and Administration in
2 consultation with Make-A-Wish Foundation of the Mid South; and
3 (2) Numbered consecutively.
4 (c) The procedures concerning costs for issuance under § 27-24-
5 1404(c)(1)(A) shall apply.
6 (d) The department shall issue a special license plate under this
7 section to a motor vehicle owner upon payment of:
8 (1) The fee required by law for registration of the motor
9 vehicle;
10 (2)(A) Thirty dollars ($30.00) to cover the design-use
11 contribution.
12 (B) The design-use contribution shall be remitted monthly
13 to Make-A-Wish Foundation of the Mid South to be used exclusively to promote
14 and support Make-A-Wish Foundation of the Mid South; and
15 (3)(A) A handling and administrative fee of ten dollars
16 ($10.00).
17 (B) The handling and administrative fee of ten dollars
18 ($10.00) shall be:
19 (i) Deposited into the State Central Services Fund
20 for the benefit of the Revenue Division of the Department of Finance and
21 Administration; and
22 (ii) Credited to the division as supplemental and in
23 addition to all other funds that may be deposited for the benefit of the
24 division.
25 (C) The handling and administrative fee shall not be
26 considered or credited to the division as direct revenue.
27 (e)(1) A special license plate issued under this section may be
28 renewed annually or replaced under the procedures set out in § 27-24-1405.
29 (2) The division shall remit the fees collected under § 27-24-
30 1405(b)(2) for the renewal of a special license plate issued under this
31 section on a monthly basis to Make-A-Wish Foundation of the Mid South.
32

34 special license plate.
35 (a) The Department of Finance and Administration shall not print,
36 produce, or acquire additional Mid-South Community College education special
