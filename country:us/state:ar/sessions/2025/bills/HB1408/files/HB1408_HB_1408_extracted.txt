24 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 20, Chapter 56, Subchapter 3, is
25 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
26 20-56-309. Medical marijuana — Health savings account — Flexible
27 spending account — Definitions.
28 (a) As used in this section:
29 (1) "Flexible spending account" means a medical expense flexible
30 spending account in conjunction with a cafeteria plan as permitted under the
31 Internal Revenue Code of 1986; and
32 (2) "Health savings account" means an account established by an
33 individual under a medical care savings account program to pay the eligible
34 medical expenses of a qualifying patient under a policy of individual or
35 group health insurance coverage that satisfies the criteria for a high-
36 deductible health plan under 26 U.S.C. § 223, as it existed on January 1,
