16 TO ALLOW SCHOOL DISTRICTS TO BILL FOR

18 ARKANSAS MEDICAID PROGRAM TO REIMBURSE

21
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
23
24 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 6-10-119, concerning Medicaid billing in
25 school districts, is amended to add an additional subsection to read as
26 follows:
27 (d)(1) A school district may bill the Arkansas Medicaid Program for
28 healthcare services provided to a student, including without limitation:
29 (A) Vision screenings;
30 (B) Hearing screenings; and
31 (C) Direct nursing services provided by a licensed
32 healthcare professional to a student under the supervision of a registered
33 nurse employed by the school district and listed in the student's
34 individualized health care plan according to § 6-18-701, including without
35 limitation to the extent allowed by federal law:
36 (i) Medication administration;
