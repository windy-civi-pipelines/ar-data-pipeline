26 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code ยง 5-27-601, concerning definitions used

HB1518
1 (a) A person commits creating, distributing, possessing, or viewing
2 artificial intelligence-generated matter depicting sexually explicit conduct
3 involving a child if the person knowingly:
4 (1) Uses artificial intelligence or other computer technology
5 capable of processing and interpreting specific data inputs to generate
6 visual depictions to create a depiction of a child engaging in sexually
7 explicit conduct that is indistinguishable from a child actually engaging in
8 sexually explicit conduct;
9 (2) Receives for the purpose of selling or knowingly sells,
10 procures, manufactures, gives, provides, lends, trades, mails, delivers,
11 transfers, publishes, distributes, circulates, disseminates, presents,
12 exhibits, advertises, offers, or agrees to offer through any means an
13 artificial intelligence-generated or computer-generated depiction that is
14 indistinguishable from a child actually engaging in sexually explicit
15 conduct; or
16 (3) Possesses or views through any means an artificial
17 intelligence-generated or computer-generated depiction that is
18 indistinguishable from a child actually engaging in sexually explicit
19 conduct.
20 (b) Creating, distributing, possessing, or viewing artificial
21 intelligence-generated matter depicting sexually explicit conduct involving a
22 child is a:
23 (1) Class C felony for the first offense; and
24 (2) Class B felony for any subsequent offense.
