24 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 14-20-108(a), concerning dues for volunteer
25 fire departments, is amended to read as follows:

27 quorum court by one (1) or more volunteer fire departments in the county
28 whose fire protection district covers an unincorporated area of the county,

30 and remit to the volunteer fire department whose fire protection district
31 covers an unincorporated area of the county the annual dues charged by the
32 volunteer fire department whose fire protection district covers an
33 unincorporated area of the county in consideration of providing fire
34 protection to those unincorporated areas in the county.

36 levy of volunteer fire department dues for a volunteer fire department whose
