19 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 14-51-301(b)(3), concerning the rules and
20 regulations governing examinations for fire departments and police
21 departments, is amended to read as follows:
22 (3)(A) Public advertisement of all examinations by publication of
23 notice in some newspaper having a bona fide circulation in the city and by
24 posting of notice at the city hall at least ten (10) days before the date of
25 the examinations.
26 (B)(i) The examinations may be held on the first Monday in April or
27 the first Monday in October, or both, and more often if necessary under such
28 rules and regulations as may be prescribed by the board shall be held as
29 needed by the chief of the fire department or police department who shall
30 determine the:
31 (a) Time of the exams;
32 (b) Date of the exams; and
33 (c) Frequency of the exams.
34 (ii) The commission shall have only an indirect role
35 in an examination under subdivision (b)(3)(B)(i) of this section, which
36 excludes the commission from:

13 termination, resignation, demotion, retirement, or promotion, the chief of

16 of an examination to establish an eligibles list from which an appointment or

22 to read as follows:
23 (7)(A) A period of probation not to exceed eighteen (18) months
24 for potential fire department appointees and at least one (1) year but no
25 longer than two (2) years for potential law enforcement appointees before any
26 appointment is complete and six (6) twelve (12) months before any promotion
27 is complete.
