15 TO REQUIRE THE ARKANSAS MEDICAID PROGRAM
16 TO COVER APPLIED BEHAVIOR ANALYSIS
17 SERVICES FOR CERTAIN BENEFICIARIES.
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
20
21 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 20, Chapter 77, Subchapter 1, is
22 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
23 20-77-154. Applied behavior analysis services.
24 (a) The Arkansas Medicaid Program shall cover applied behavior
25 analysis services for beneficiaries who:
26 (1) Are over twenty-one (21) years of age;
27 (2) Have a diagnosis of autism;
28 (3) Are participants in the Community and Employment Services
29 Waiver; and
30 (4) Have received a determination from a primary care physician,
31 attending psychologist, or psychiatrist that applied behavior analysis
32 services are medically necessary.
33 (b) The applied behavior analysis services shall be delivered:
34 (1) Based on the needs of the beneficiary, including applied
35 behavior analysis services on a one-to-one basis at home and in the
36 community; and
