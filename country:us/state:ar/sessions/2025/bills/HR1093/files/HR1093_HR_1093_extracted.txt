15 TO RECOGNIZE THE IZARD COUNTY HIGH
16 SCHOOL LADY COUGARS BASKETBALL TEAM AS
17 THE 2025 CLASS 2A STATE CHAMPIONS.
18
19 WHEREAS, the Izard County High School Lady Cougars basketball team won
20 the 2025 Class 2A state championship after defeating the Barton High School
21 Lady Bears by a score of seventy-two (72) to sixty-six (66) points on March
22 13, 2025, at Bank OZK Arena in Hot Springs, Arkansas; and
23
24 WHEREAS, the Lady Cougars earned their spot in the state championship
25 game by winning their last twenty-four (24) games of the season and defeating
26 the top four (4) ranked teams in the state during the regional and state
27 tournaments; and
28
29 WHEREAS, the Lady Cougars completed the season with an overall record
30 of thirty-two wins and four losses (32-4) and won the, the George Kell
31 Memorial Classic, the 2A-2 conference co-championship, the 2A-2 district
32 tournament, the 2A Region 2 tournament, and the Class 2A state championship
33 tournament; and
34
35 WHEREAS, Lady Cougars team members included Quinn Johnson, Olivia
36 Spray, Makensie Yancey, Tinley Bailey, Emily Stephens, Kallie Skidmore,

14
15 WHEREAS, the Lady Cougars were led by Head Coach David Harmon and
16 Assistant Coach Kyle McCandlis; and
17
18 WHEREAS, Head Coach David Harmon has achieved the rare feat of coaching
19 both a boys' state championship team in 2008 and a girls' state championship
20 team in 2025 for the same high school,
21
22 NOW THEREFORE,
23 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
24 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
25
26 THAT the House of Representatives recognize the Izard County High
27 School Lady Cougars basketball team as the 2025 Class 2A state champions.
28
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
30 presented to Head Coach David Harmon by the Chief Clerk of the House of
31 Representatives.
