Subtitle

14 TO AMEND THE LAW CONCERNING LICENSES TO
15 CARRY CONCEALED HANDGUNS.
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
18
19 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 5-73-312(a)(2), concerning revocation of a
20 license to carry a concealed handgun, is amended to read as follows:
21 (2)(A) Any law enforcement officer making an arrest of a
22 licensee for a violation of this subchapter or any other statutory violation
23 that requires revocation of a license to carry a concealed handgun shall
24 confiscate the license and forward it to notify the Director of the Division
25 of Arkansas State Police.
26 (B) The license shall be held until a determination of the
27 charge is finalized, with the appropriate disposition of the license after
28 the determination.
29
30 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 5-73-314 is amended to read as follows:
31 5-73-314. Lost, destroyed, or duplicate license — Change of address.
32 (a) Within thirty (30) days after the changing of a permanent address,
33 or within thirty (30) days after having a license to carry a concealed
34 handgun lost, the licensee shall notify the Director of the Division of
35 Arkansas State Police in writing of the change or loss.
36 (b) If a license to carry a concealed handgun is lost or destroyed, or
