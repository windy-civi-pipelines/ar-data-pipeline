17 TO RECOGNIZE FEBRUARY 15-22, 2025, AS
18 NATIONAL FFA WEEK.
19
20 WHEREAS, the National FFA Organization and agricultural education
21 provide a strong foundation for the youth of America and the future of the
22 food, fiber, and natural resources systems; and
23
24 WHEREAS, the Arkansas Division of the Future Farmers of America
25 officers and the National FFA Organization officers serve as role models who
26 inspire and encourage members to achieve premier leadership, personal growth,
27 and career success; and
28
29 WHEREAS, the Arkansas Division of the Future Farmers of America
30 currently has seventeen thousand five hundred three (17,503) members, with
31 two hundred seventeen (217) local chapters and three hundred thirty-five
32 (335) advisors; and
33
34 WHEREAS, agricultural education and the National FFA Organization
35 ensure a steady supply of young professionals to meet growing demand in the
36 field of the science, business, and technology of agriculture; and

18 THAT the Senate recognize the importance and positive work of the

22
