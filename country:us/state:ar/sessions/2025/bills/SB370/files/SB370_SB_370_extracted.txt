23
24 SECTION 1. DO NOT CODIFY. Cabinet-level department transfer of the
25 Arkansas Wine Producers Council from Department of Commerce to Department of
26 Parks, Heritage, and Tourism.
27 (a) The Arkansas Wine Producers Council is transferred by a cabinet-
28 level department transfer under § 25-43-101 et seq. from the Department of
29 Commerce to the Tourism Division of the Department of Parks, Heritage, and
30 Tourism.
31 (b) The administrative functions of the Arkansas Wine Producers
32 Council are transferred by a cabinet-level department transfer under § 25-43-
33 105 from the Department of Commerce to the Tourism Division of the Department
34 of Parks, Heritage, and Tourism.
35 (c) The statutory authority, powers, duties, functions, records,
36 personnel, property, contracts, and unexpended balances of appropriations,

1 allocations, or other funds, including the functions of budgeting or
2 purchasing of the Arkansas Wine Producers Council, are transferred to the
3 Tourism Division of the Department of Parks, Heritage, and Tourism.
4 (d)(1) The transfer of the Arkansas Wine Producers Council does not
5 affect the orders, rules, directives, or standards made or promulgated by the
6 Arkansas Wine Producers Council before the effective date of this act.
7 (2) The orders, rules, directives, or standards under
8 subdivision (d)(1) of this section shall continue to be in effect until
9 amended or repealed under authority given by law.
10 (e) The Department of Commerce shall grant access to and provide all
11 information requested by the Tourism Division of the Department of Parks,
12 Heritage, and Tourism to accomplish the transfer of the Arkansas Wine
13 Producers Council and the mission of the Arkansas Wine Producers Council.
14
15 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 3-5-701(a)(1), concerning the creation of
16 the Arkansas Wine Producers Council, is amended to read as follows:
17 (a)(1) There is created within the Tourism Division of the Department
18 of Parks, Heritage, and Tourism the Arkansas Wine Producers Council, to be
19 composed of seven (7) members.
20
21 SECTION 3. Arkansas Code § 3-5-703(d), concerning the powers and
22 duties of the Arkansas Wine Producers Council, is amended to read as follows:
23 (d) Upon approval by a majority vote of the council of a proposal for
24 research by a university research institution or for promotion or tourism by
25 the commission, the council shall direct the Chief Fiscal Officer of the
26 State to transfer on the Department of Finance and Administration books, and
27 shall cause to be transferred on the books of the Treasurer of State and the
28 Auditor of State, such amounts as determined by the council from the Arkansas
29 Wine Producers Council Fund to the Department of Commerce Department of
30 Parks, Heritage, and Tourism Fund Account and to the operating fund or fund
31 accounts of approved research institutions. Use of these funds may be applied
32 as prescribed in this section in the various states of the United States and
33 foreign countries.

27 SECTION 7. EMERGENCY CLAUSE. It is found and determined by the
28 General Assembly of the State of Arkansas that the Arkansas Wine Producers
29 Council preserves the public peace, health, and safety by providing needed
30 funds and services for Arkansas; that this act provides for the transfer of
31 the Arkansas Wine Producers Council to the Department of Parks, Heritage, and
32 Tourism; and that this act should become effective on July 1, 2025, to
33 coincide with the appropriation bills of the Department of Commerce and the
34 Department of Parks, Heritage, and Tourism and ensure that the Arkansas Wine
35 Producers Council continues to provide its vital services as the transfer is
36 implemented and does not experience any issues with funding under the
