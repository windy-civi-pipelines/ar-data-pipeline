15 TO ALLOW A DEDUCTION FOR CERTAIN
16 QUALIFIED BUSINESS EXPENSES UNDER THE
17 INCOME TAX ACT OF 1929.
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
20
21 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 26, Chapter 51, Subchapter 4, is
22 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
23 26-51-462. Deduction — Qualified business expenses.
24 Title 26 U.S.C. § 199A(a)-(h), as in effect on January 1, 2025,
25 regarding the deduction for qualified business income, is adopted for the
26 purpose of computing Arkansas income tax liability.
27
28 SECTION 2. EFFECTIVE DATE. Section 1 of this act is effective for tax
29 years beginning on or after January 1, 2025.
