18 SECTION 7. EMERGENCY CLAUSE. It is found and determined by the
19 General Assembly of the State of Arkansas that the State Board of Appraisers,
20 Abstracters, and Home Inspectors preserves the public peace, health, and
21 safety by providing needed funds and services for Arkansas; that this act
22 provides for the abolition and transfer of the Arkansas Abstracters' Board
23 Fund and the Arkansas Home Inspectors Registration Fund to the State Board of
24 Appraisers, Abstracters, and Home Inspectors Fund; and that this act should
25 become effective on July 1, 2025, to coincide with the appropriation bill of
26 the State Board of Appraisers, Abstracters, and Home Inspectors and ensure
27 that the State Board of Appraisers, Abstracters, and Home Inspectors
28 continues to provide its vital services as the transfer is implemented and
29 does not experience any issues with funding under the transfer. Therefore, an
30 emergency is declared to exist, and this act being necessary for the
31 preservation of the public peace, health, and safety shall become effective
32 on July 1, 2025.

18 SECTION 7. EMERGENCY CLAUSE. It is found and determined by the
19 General Assembly of the State of Arkansas that the State Board of Appraisers,
20 Abstracters, and Home Inspectors preserves the public peace, health, and
21 safety by providing needed funds and services for Arkansas; that this act
22 provides for the abolition and transfer of the Arkansas Abstracters' Board
23 Fund and the Arkansas Home Inspectors Registration Fund to the State Board of
24 Appraisers, Abstracters, and Home Inspectors Fund; and that this act should
25 become effective on July 1, 2025, to coincide with the appropriation bill of
26 the State Board of Appraisers, Abstracters, and Home Inspectors and ensure
27 that the State Board of Appraisers, Abstracters, and Home Inspectors
28 continues to provide its vital services as the transfer is implemented and
29 does not experience any issues with funding under the transfer. Therefore, an
30 emergency is declared to exist, and this act being necessary for the
31 preservation of the public peace, health, and safety shall become effective
32 on July 1, 2025.
