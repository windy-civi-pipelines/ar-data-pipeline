24 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 7, Chapter 5, Subchapter 3, is amended
25 to add an additional section to read as follows:
26 7-5-325. Designated restrooms for campaign workers and volunteers.
27 (a) A county board of election commissioners may designate restroom
28 facilities to be used by campaign workers or volunteers at a polling site.
29 (b) A campaign worker or volunteer working outside of the polling site
30 may use restroom facilities in the polling site designated by the county
31 board of election commissioners under subsection (a) of this section.
32 (c) Before entering the polling site to use the designated restroom
33 facilities under this section, a campaign worker or volunteer:
34 (1) Shall cover or remove campaign material advocating voting
35 for or against a:
36 (A) Candidate; or
