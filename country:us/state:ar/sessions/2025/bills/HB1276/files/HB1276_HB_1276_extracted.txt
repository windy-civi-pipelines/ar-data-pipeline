Subtitle

18 CONCERNING SPOUSAL RETIREMENT BENEFITS
19 FOR SPOUSES OF CERTAIN MUNICIPAL
20 EMPLOYEES.
21
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
23
24 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 24-12-120, concerning retirement benefits
25 for city attorneys in cities of the first class and cities of the second
26 class, is amended to add an additional subsection to read as follows:
27 (d)(1) Upon the death of a city attorney who retired under the
28 provisions of subsection (a) of this section or a city attorney who dies in
29 office after becoming eligible to retire under subsection (a) of this section
30 or any other state statute, the legally recognized spouse of the city
31 attorney married to the city attorney for ten (10) years or longer may
32 receive, at the option of the governing body of the city:
33 (A) One-half (1/2) of the retirement benefit the retired
34 city attorney was receiving; or
35 (B) One-half (1/2) of the retirement benefit the city
36 attorney who died in office was entitled to receive.
