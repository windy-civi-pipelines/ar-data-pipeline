16 TO AMEND THE LAW CONCERNING THE DUTIES
17 OF A COUNTY PROPERTY ASSESSOR; AND TO
18 PERMIT A PROPERTY OWNER TO OPT OUT OF

20 POSTED ON AN ONLINE DATABASE.

22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
23
24 SECTION 1. DO NOT CODIFY. Legislative findings.
25 The General Assembly finds that:
26 (1) Records published online by county assessors are for the
27 convenience of a property owner;
28 (2) Online records afford a property owner the ability to pay
29 taxes and fees online;
30 (3) County assessors in Arkansas are not required by law to
31 publish records online however, certain records are available upon request;
32 and
33 (4) Property owners may desire to opt out of having their
34 records placed online for privacy or other reasons.
