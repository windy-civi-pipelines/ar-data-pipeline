20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
22
23 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 25-19-103(6), concerning the
24 definition of "public meeting" under the Freedom of Information Act of 1967,
25 is amended to read as follows:
26 (6) “Public meetings” means the meetings of more than two (2)
27 members of any bureau, commission, or agency of the state or any political
28 subdivision of the state, including municipalities and counties, boards of
29 education, and all other boards, bureaus, commissions, or organizations in
30 the State of Arkansas, except grand juries, supported wholly or in part by
31 public funds or expending public funds;
32
33 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 25-19-106(a), concerning public meetings
34 under the Freedom of Information Act of 1967, is amended to read as follows:
35 (a)(1) Except as otherwise specifically provided by law, all meetings,
36 formal or informal, special or regular, of more than two (2) members of the
