24 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:

26 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 7-5-615(d), concerning the tabulation of
27 votes and damaged or defective ballots, is amended to read as follows:
28 (d)(1) If any ballot is damaged or defective so that it cannot
29 properly be counted by the electronic vote tabulating device, a true
30 duplicate copy shall be made of the damaged ballot.
31 (2) When duplicating a ballot, and in the presence of another
32 election official and designated poll watchers, if any, an election official
33 shall:
34 (A) Reproduce the selections of the voter on a second
35 ballot that is the same ballot style as the ballot cast by the voter;
36 (B) Stamp or write the word “duplicate” on the duplicated

1 ballot;

4 (D) Label both the original damaged or defective ballot
5 and the duplicate ballot with the same unique serial number;
6 (E) Substitute the duplicated ballot for the original
7 damaged or defective ballot;
8 (F) Count the duplicated ballots in the same manner as
9 other ballots in that election; and
10 (G) Secure and separately store the original damaged or
11 defective ballots in the same manner as other ballots in that election.

13 the central counting facility shall be conducted by an election official at

15 (4) The duplication of a damaged or defective ballot cast at a
16 polling site shall be delivered securely to the county board of election
17 commissioners to be duplicated by election officials or added to the
18 precinct's count manually on the night of the election.
19 (5)(4) An election official under subdivision (d)(2) of this
20 section shall maintain a recorded count of damaged ballots to be duplicated
21 in the presence of authorized poll watchers, if any, and provide the report
22 to the county board of election commissioners.

24 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 7-5-615, concerning the tabulation of votes
25 and damaged or defective ballots, is amended to add an additional subsection
26 to read as follows:
27 (g) The State Board of Election Commissioners shall promulgate
28 necessary rules regarding the duplication of damaged or defective ballots
29 under this section.
30
