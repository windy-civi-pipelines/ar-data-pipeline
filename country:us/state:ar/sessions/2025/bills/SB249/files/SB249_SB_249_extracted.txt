16 TO AMEND THE LAW CONCERNING VIOLATIONS
17 AND PENALTIES RELATED TO FORESTRY; AND
18 TO AMEND THE POWERS OF THE ARKANSAS
19 FORESTRY COMMISSION.
20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
22
23 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 17-31-105(a), concerning violations and
24 penalties related to forestry, is amended to read as follows:
25 (a)(1) Any person required to be registered under this chapter shall
26 be guilty of a Class A misdemeanor if that person The Arkansas Forestry
27 Commission may impose a civil penalty not to exceed five thousand dollars
28 ($5,000) on a person required to be registered under this chapter if that
29 person:
30 (1)(A) Practices or offers to practice forestry without a
31 valid certificate of registration issued pursuant to this chapter;
32 (2)(B) Claims to be a forester certified under this chapter
33 without being so certified;
34 (3)(C) In any way tends to convey the impression that he or
35 she is a forester certified under the provisions of this chapter without
36 being so registered;
