18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:

20 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 7, Chapter 1, Subchapter 1, is amended
21 to add an additional section to read as follows:
22 7-1-116. Ranked choice voting prohibited.
23 (a) As used in this section, "ranked choice voting" means a voting
24 method that:
25 (1) Allows a voter to rank candidates for an office in order of
26 preference; and
27 (2) Has the ballots cast tabulated in multiple rounds following
28 the elimination of a candidate until a single candidate attains a majority
29 vote.
30 (b) Ranked choice voting shall not be used to determine the election
31 or nomination of a candidate to any local, state, or federal elective office
32 in this state.
33 (c) An existing or future ordinance enacted or adopted by a county, a
34 municipality, or other local governmental entity that is in conflict with
35 this section is void.
36 (d) This section shall not apply to a qualified elector who is
