17 SECTION 15. EMERGENCY CLAUSE. It is found and determined by the
18 General Assembly of the State of Arkansas that the management of premium
19 taxes collected for the benefit of municipal firemen's relief and pension
20 funds and policemen's pension and relief funds is in need of a change in
21 administration from the Arkansas Fire and Police Pension Review Board to the
22 Department of Finance and Administration for purposes of financial security
23 and efficiency; and that it is necessary for this act to have an effective
24 date of July 1, 2025, in order to allow for a structured and proper
25 administration of the procedures referenced in this act. Therefore, an
26 emergency is declared to exist, and this act being necessary for the
27 preservation of the public peace, health, and safety shall become effective
28 on July 1, 2025.
