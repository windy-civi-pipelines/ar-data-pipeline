Subtitle
14
15 TO RECOGNIZE THE YELLVILLE-SUMMIT
16 PANTHERS GIRL'S TRACK AND FIELD TEAM AS
17 THE 2024 CLASS 2A STATE CHAMPIONS.
18
19 WHEREAS, the Yellville-Summit Panthers girl's track and field team won
20 the Class 2A State Track Meet held in Quitman on Tuesday, April 30, 2024; and
21
22 WHEREAS, this was the fourth straight Class 2A state championship win
23 for the Panthers; and
24
25 WHEREAS, the Panthers won these four (4) championships without having a
26 track and field facility, and they practice the track portion of the sport by
27 running at the Yellville City Park; and
28
29 WHEREAS, K.J. Moore led the team by either winning or sharing victory
30 in six (6) different events; and
31
32 WHEREAS, K.J. Moore successfully defended her titles from the prior
33 year in the four-hundred-meter dash and the high jump; and
34
35 WHEREAS, K.J. Moore also won the long jump, the one-hundred-meter
36 hurdles, and the two-hundred-meter dash; and

2 WHEREAS, K.J. Moore joined Bailey Ervin, Hannah Hayward, and Abby
3 Methvin in winning the four-by-four hundred meter relay, which was the second
4 straight title in this event for Moore, Ervin, and Methvin and the fourth
5 straight title in this event for Moore and Methvin; and
6
7 WHEREAS, the Panthers were also represented by Riley Hopson, who won
8 the eight-hundred-meter and sixteen-hundred-meter runs; and
9
10 WHEREAS, the Panthers were also represented by Fay Chism, who placed
11 second in pole vault and fourth in the three-hundred-meter hurdles; and
12
13 WHEREAS, the Panthers finished thirty-five (35) points ahead of the
14 runners-up from Gurdon; and
15
16 WHEREAS, the Panthers were led by Head Coach April Martin, who was
17 named the Section 6 Girls' Outdoor Track and Field Coach of the Year by the
18 National Federation of State High School Associations and the 2024 Arkansas
19 Democrat-Gazette All-Arkansas Preps Girl's Track Coach of the Year; and
20
21 WHEREAS, Head Coach April Martin stated, "We don't have the best
22 facilities, but that doesn't hold us back. We're going to work hard every day
23 that we can",
24
25 NOW THEREFORE,
26 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
27 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
28
29 THAT the House of Representatives recognize the Yellville-Summit
30 Panthers girl's track and field team as the 2024 Class 2A state champions.
31
32 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
33 presented to Head Coach April Martin by the Chief Clerk of the House of
34 Representatives.
