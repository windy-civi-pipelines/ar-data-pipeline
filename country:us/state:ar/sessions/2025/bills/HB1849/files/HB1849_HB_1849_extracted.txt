22
23 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 12, Chapter 6, is amended to add an
24 additional subchapter to read as follows:
25 Subchapter 9 — Tribal Lands
26
27 12-16-901. Cross deputization.
28 (a) A municipality, a county, or the state may enter into an agreement
29 with a tribal nation having federally-recognized tribal land within the
30 borders of the state for the cross deputization of law enforcement agencies.
31 (b) An agreement under subsection (a) of this section may allow
32 municipal, county, state law and tribal law enforcement agencies to have
33 authority to enforce tribal, state, and local laws within the borders of the
34 state on federally-recognized tribal land and surrounding jurisdictions.
