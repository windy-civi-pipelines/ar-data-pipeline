20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
21
22 SECTION 1. DO NOT CODIFY. Title.
23 This act shall be known and may be cited as "The Water in Line Act of
24 2025".
25
26 SECTION 2. DO NOT CODIFY. Legislative findings and intent.
27 The General Assembly finds that:
28 (1) Arkansas has a longstanding law prohibiting electioneering
29 within one hundred feet (100') of a polling place;
30 (2) Act 728 of 2021 applies to people who are not engaging in
31 electioneering activities;
32 (3) In certain circumstances, Act 728 of 2021 makes it a
33 criminal offense for a volunteer to bring water or food to someone waiting in
34 line to vote, a family member to accompany an elderly or disabled voter, and
35 a child to join his or her parent in line to vote; and
36 (4) The repeal of Act 728 of 2021 would have no effect on Arkansas's

3 SECTION 3. Arkansas Code § 7-1-103(a)(24), concerning miscellaneous
4 misdemeanor offenses relating to elections, is repealed.
5 (24) A person shall not enter or remain in an area within one
6 hundred feet (100′) of the primary exterior entrance to a building where
