14 TO ELIMINATE FUNDING TO THE ARKANSAS
15 CENTER FOR HEALTH IMPROVEMENT.
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
18
19 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 25, Chapter 1, Subchapter 1, is amended
20 to add an additional section to read as follows:
21 25-1-130. Funding of Arkansas Center for Health Improvement by state
22 moneys prohibited — Definition.
23 (a) As used in this section, "state agency" means every department,
24 division, office, board, commission, and institution of this state, including
25 state-supported institutions of higher education and state-supported medical
26 facilities.
27 (b)(1) No state moneys shall be used by a state agency to provide
28 funding or grants or to enter into a contract with the Arkansas Center for
29 Health Improvement.
30 (2) A state agency that provides funding or grants or enters
31 into a contract with the Arkansas Center for Health Improvement shall execute
32 a written certification that no state moneys are being used for the funding,
33 grant, or contract.
34
