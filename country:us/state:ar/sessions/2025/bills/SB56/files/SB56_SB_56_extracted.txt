20 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code ยง 25-15-401(c), concerning the reporting,
21 sunsetting, and extension of rules, is amended to read as follows:
22 (c)(1) The agencies shall be divided into six (6) rule review groups to
23 be determined by the Governor to ensure that the number of agencies in each

26 (2) If a new agency is created, the Governor shall assign the new
27 agency to a rule review group.
28 (3)(A) The Governor may amend the division of agencies into rule
29 review groups.
30 (B) An amendment to the division of agencies into rule
31 review groups on or after the effective date of this subdivision (c)(3) shall
32 not result in the rules of an agency being evaluated more than twelve (12)
33 years from their last evaluation under this section.
