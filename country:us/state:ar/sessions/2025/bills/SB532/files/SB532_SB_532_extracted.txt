16 TO AMEND THE DEFINITION OF "SPECIALTY
17 HOSPITAL" RELATING TO THE ASSESSMENT FEE
18 ON HOSPITALS UNDER THE ARKANSAS MEDICAID
19 PROGRAM.
20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
22
23 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 20-77-1901(7), concerning the definition of
24 "specialty hospital" relating to the assessment fee on hospitals, is amended
25 to read as follows:
26 (7) “Specialty hospital” means an acute care general hospital
27 that:
28 (A) Limits services primarily to children and qualifies as
29 exempt from the Medicare prospective payment system regulation; or
30 (B) Is primarily or exclusively engaged in the care and
31 treatment of patients with:
32 (i) Cardiac cardiac conditions; or
33 (ii) Orthopedic or spinal conditions;
