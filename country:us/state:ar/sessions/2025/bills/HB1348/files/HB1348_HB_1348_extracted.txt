26 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 24-4-608(d)(2), regarding benefits for a

33 (B)(i) However, the age-eighteen maximum shall be extended
34 as long as the child continues uninterruptedly being a full-time student at
35 an accredited secondary school, college, or university, but in no event
36 beyond his or her attainment of twenty-three (23) years of age.

29 SECTION 5. EMERGENCY CLAUSE. It is found and determined by the
30 General Assembly of the State of Arkansas that certain provisions of the law
31 related to the extension of benefits to surviving children of members of the
32 Arkansas Public Employees' Retirement System and the State Police Retirement
33 System are in need of revision and updating to streamline the provision of
34 support for beneficiaries; that the respective retirement systems operate on
35 a fiscal calendar year of July 1 to June 30; that having a July 1, 2025,
36 effective date is necessary to allow the provisions within this act to begin
3 03-12-2025 14:04:29 LJH118

1 on the first day of the fiscal year to provide proper administration of the
2 procedures herein; that such revisions and updating are of great importance
3 for actuarial purposes and to protect the benefits to members of the Arkansas
4 Public Employees’ Retirement System and the State Police Retirement System;
5 and that this act is immediately necessary in order to maintain an orderly
6 system of benefits for the members of the Arkansas Public Employees’
7 Retirement System and the State Police Retirement System. Therefore, an
8 emergency is declared to exist and this act being necessary for the
9 preservation of the public peace, health, and safety shall become effective
10 on July 1, 2025.
