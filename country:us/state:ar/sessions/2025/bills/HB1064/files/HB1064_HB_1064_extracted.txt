20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
21
22 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 7-5-402 is amended to read as follows:
23 7-5-402. Voter qualification.
24 The following persons, if possessing the qualifications of electors,
25 may cast an absentee ballot in any election:
26 (1) Any person who will be unavoidably absent from his or her
27 voting place on the day of the election;
28 (2) Any person who will be unable to attend the polls on
29 election day because of illness or physical disability; and
30 (3) Any person who is prevented from voting due to observance of
31 a religious discipline or religious holiday during the entire twelve (12)
32 hours that the polls are open on election day.; and
33 (4) Any person who is over sixty-five (65) years of age on
34 election day.
35
