19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
20
21 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 20, Chapter 77, Subchapter 1, is
22 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
23 20-77-154. Extension of one year for postpartum coverage.
24 (a) The Department of Human Services shall:
25 (1)(A) Extend postpartum Medicaid coverage for mothers to one
26 (1) year in duration.
27 (B) If a mother is eligible to be enrolled under the
28 Arkansas Health and Opportunity for Me Act of 2021, § 23-61-1001 et seq., the
29 extended postpartum Medicaid coverage shall be delivered as provided under
30 the Arkansas Health and Opportunity for Me Act of 2021,§ 23-61-1001 et seq.
31 (C) If a mother is not eligible to be enrolled under the
32 Arkansas Health and Opportunity for Me Act of 2021, § 23-61-1001 et seq., the
33 extended postpartum Medicaid coverage shall be delivered through the
34 traditional Arkansas Medicaid Program;
35 (2) Take necessary action, including without limitation
36 submitting and applying for a state plan amendment or waiver, to obtain
