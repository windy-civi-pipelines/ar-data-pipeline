19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:

21 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 15, Chapter 23, Subchapter 1, is
22 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
23 15-23-107. Prohibition on moratorium in watersheds and other bodies of
24 water.
25 (a) Except as provided in subsection (b) of this section, a state
26 agency, including without limitation the Department of Agriculture and the
27 Department of Energy and Environment, shall not institute a moratorium on the
28 issuance of permits in a watershed, including without limitation the Buffalo
29 River Watershed, or other body of water.
30 (b) If a state agency wishes to institute a moratorium related to a
31 watershed or other body of water, the state agency first shall obtain
32 approval from the Legislative Council.
33
