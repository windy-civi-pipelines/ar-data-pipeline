16 TO ENSURE THAT MANDATED COSTS AND FEES

22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
23
24 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 16, Chapter 92, Subchapter 1, is
25 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
26 16-92-107. Mandated costs and fees — Defendant refunded — Definition.
27 (a) As used in this section, "mandated costs and fees" includes
28 without limitation any sum that is required to be paid by the defendant that
29 is associated with being subject to a prosecution, including without
30 limitation court filing costs and fees, service costs and fees, ignition
31 interlock device costs and fees, pretrial supervision costs and fees, and
32 ankle monitor costs and fees.
33 (b) Any mandated costs and fees paid by a defendant in a criminal case
34 shall be refunded to the defendant if:
35 (1) He or she is acquitted of the charge or charges; or
36 (2) The charge or charges are otherwise dismissed or an order of

5 only the mandated costs and fees paid by the defendant that are specifically
