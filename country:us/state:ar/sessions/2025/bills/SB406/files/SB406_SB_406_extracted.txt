22 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code ยง 14-15-403, concerning instruments affecting
23 title to property, is amended to add an additional subsection to read as
24 follows:
25 (f)(1)(A)(i) A deed to real estate presented to a recorder for
26 recording either in person or by mail shall not be received for record or
27 filing by the recorder unless the grantor named in the deed to real estate or
28 an individual signing the deed on behalf of the grantor named in the deed to
29 real estate appears in person at the office of the recorder and presents a
30 valid photo identification card or driver's license.
31 (ii) If a deed to real estate is presented to a
32 recorder for recording by mail, the grantor named in the deed to the real
33 estate shall include a photocopy of his or her valid photo identification or
34 driver's license with the deed to real estate submitted by mail.

36 "valid photo identification card or driver's license" means a photo
