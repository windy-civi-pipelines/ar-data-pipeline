17 SECTION 7. SPECIAL LANGUAGE. NOT TO BE INCORPORATED INTO THE ARKANSAS
18 CODE NOR PUBLISHED SEPARATELY AS SPECIAL, LOCAL AND TEMPORARY LAW. FUND
19 TRANSFER. To provide funding for the appropriations authorized herein, the
20 Director of the Department of Finance and Administration - Assessment
21 Coordination Division of the State of Arkansas shall certify monthly to the
22 Chief Fiscal Officer of the State, the amount of funding needed each month to
23 pay counties and professional reappraisal companies for the reappraisal of
24 real property as required by law. Upon receipt of such certification the
25 Chief Fiscal Officer of the State shall transfer on his or her books and
26 those of the State Treasurer 76% of the amounts certified from the Department
27 of Education Public School Fund Account, 16% of the amount certified from the
28 County Aid Fund, and 8% of the amount certified from the Municipal Aid Fund
29 to the Arkansas Real Property Reappraisal Fund. In no event shall the total
30 amount of funds transferred as authorized herein exceed fourteen million two
31 hundred fifty thousand dollars ($14,250,000) in a single fiscal year.
32 The provisions of this section shall be in effect only from July 1, 2024
33 2025 through June 30, 2025 2026.

5 SECTION 12. EMERGENCY CLAUSE. It is found and determined by the
6 General Assembly, that the Constitution of the State of Arkansas prohibits
7 the appropriation of funds for more than a one (1) year period; that the
8 effectiveness of this Act on July 1, 2025 is essential to the operation of
9 the agency for which the appropriations in this Act are provided, and that in
10 the event of an extension of the legislative session, the delay in the
11 effective date of this Act beyond July 1, 2025 could work irreparable harm
12 upon the proper administration and provision of essential governmental
13 programs. Therefore, an emergency is hereby declared to exist and this Act
14 being necessary for the immediate preservation of the public peace, health
15 and safety shall be in full force and effect from and after July 1, 2025.
