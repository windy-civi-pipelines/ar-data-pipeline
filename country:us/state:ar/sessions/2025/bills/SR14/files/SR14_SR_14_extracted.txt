14 TO RECOGNIZE FEBRUARY 9-15, 2025, AS
15 EUREKA SPRINGS FBLA WEEK.
16
17 WHEREAS, Future Business Leaders of America, Inc., also known as FBLA,
18 is a nonprofit educational organization whose first high school chapter was
19 established in Johnson City, Tennessee, in 1942; and
20
21 WHEREAS, Iowa became the first state chapter of Future Business Leaders
22 of America, Inc., in 1947; and
23
24 WHEREAS, Eureka Springs High School officially chartered its local
25 chapter of Future Business Leaders of America, Inc., on October 31, 1972; and
26
27 WHEREAS, Future Business Leaders of America, Inc., includes more than
28 two hundred thousand (200,000) members and advisers in four thousand six
29 hundred (4,600) chapters nationwide in middle schools, high schools,
30 colleges, universities, career and technical schools, and private business
31 schools; and
32
33 WHEREAS, Future Business Leaders of America, Inc., is a professional
34 business organization dedicated to bringing business and education together
35 in a positive working relationship through innovative leadership and career
36 development programs; and

2 WHEREAS, Future Business Leaders of America, Inc., members perform
3 community service activities and strive to build a student's understanding of
4 the realities of the modern business world; and
5
6 WHEREAS, Future Business Leaders of America, Inc., teaches middle
7 school, high school, and college students basic business and leadership
8 principles and assists them in the transition from school to work; and
9
10 WHEREAS, the Eureka Springs chapter of Future Business Leaders of
11 America, Inc., is an active chapter and participated in the food drive this
12 past holiday season to provide meals for thirty-two (32) families this past
13 winter; and
14
15 WHEREAS, the Eureka Springs chapter of Future Business Leaders of
16 America, Inc., is a Champion Chapter with the Bronze designation,
17
18 NOW THEREFORE,
19 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE
20 STATE OF ARKANSAS:
21
22 THAT the Senate recognize the importance and positive work and commend
23 the leaders of the Eureka Springs chapter of Future Business Leaders of
24 America, Inc., and recognize February 9-15, 2025, as Eureka Springs FBLA
25 Week.
26
27 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
28 provided to the Eureka Springs chapter of Future Business Leaders of America,
29 Inc., by the Secretary of the Senate.
