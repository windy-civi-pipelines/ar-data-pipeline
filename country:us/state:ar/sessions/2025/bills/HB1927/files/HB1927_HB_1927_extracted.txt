5 SECTION 2. EMERGENCY CLAUSE. It is found and determined by the
6 General Assembly of the State of Arkansas that the office of the Secretary of
7 State provides vital services, including without limitation the regulation of
8 elections and the registration of business entities, that preserve the public
9 peace, health, and safety; that due to an error in Acts 2025, No. 408,

11 the end of the current fiscal year; and that the immediate effectiveness of
12 this act is necessary to preserve the public peace, health, and safety as it
13 will ensure that funding for the office is in place at the beginning of the
14 next fiscal year and allow proper planning for the fiscal operations of the
15 office. Therefore, an emergency is declared to exist, and this act being
16 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health, and
17 safety shall become effective on:
18 (1) The date of its approval by the Governor;

20 the expiration of the period of time during which the Governor may veto the
21 bill; or
22 (3) If the bill is vetoed by the Governor and the veto is
23 overridden, the date the last house overrides the veto.
