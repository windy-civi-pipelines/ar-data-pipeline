25 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 6, Chapter 80, is amended to add an
26 additional section to read as follows:
27 6-80-111. Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program information.
28 (a) The Secretary of the Department of Education shall send to each
29 student who submits a Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) and
30 who has a negative or zero (0) student aid index for the year written and
31 electronic information regarding:
32 (1) His or her potential eligibility for assistance under the
33 Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program;
34 (2) The application process to obtain assistance under the
35 Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program; and
36 (3) How to contact the Department of Human Services regarding
