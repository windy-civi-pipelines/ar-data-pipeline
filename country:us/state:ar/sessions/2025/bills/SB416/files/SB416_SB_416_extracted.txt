20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
21
22 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 15-10-101 is repealed.
23 15-10-101. Electric Vehicle Infrastructure Grant Program.
24 (a) There is created within the Department of Energy and Environment
25 the Electric Vehicle Infrastructure Grant Program to be administered by the
26 Secretary of the Department of Energy and Environment.
27 (b) The secretary has exclusive authority to award grants to public or
28 private entities under the program to be used for construction, installation,
29 and associated costs connected with the deployment of Level 2 and Level 3
30 charging facilities.
31
32 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 19-5-1273 is repealed.
33 19-5-1273. Electric Vehicle Infrastructure Fund.
34 (a) There is created on the books of the Treasurer of State, the
35 Auditor of State, and the Chief Fiscal Officer of the State a miscellaneous
36 fund to be known as the “Electric Vehicle Infrastructure Fund”.
