16 TO RECOGNIZE AND SUPPORT THE ROLE
17 ADVANCED ENERGY PLAYS IN SECURING ENERGY
18 INDEPENDENCE, FOSTERING ECONOMIC
19 PROSPERITY, AND ENHANCING THE STATE’S
20 ENERGY RESILIENCE.
21
22 WHEREAS, Arkansas has a strong tradition of energy leadership, and
23 reliable power generation, grid modernization, energy efficiency, nuclear
24 energy, and domestic energy production ensure the state’s energy security,
25 economic growth, and long-term prosperity; and
26
27 WHEREAS, energy independence is essential to Arkansas’s economic
28 strength, reducing reliance on external energy sources and empowering
29 businesses, farmers, and households with reliable, affordable, and locally
30 generated power; and
31
32 WHEREAS, advanced energy industries contribute significantly to
33 Arkansas’s economy by attracting investment, supporting agriculture and
34 manufacturing, and providing cost-effective energy solutions to businesses,
35 schools, and municipalities; and
36

5 WHEREAS, Arkansas recognizes the importance of balancing energy

18
19 THAT the House of Representatives recognize and support the role
20 advanced energy plays in securing energy independence, fostering economic
21 prosperity, and enhancing the state’s energy resilience.
