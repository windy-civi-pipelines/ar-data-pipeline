3 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code Title 26, Chapter 57, Subchapter 2, is
4 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
5 26-57-269. Prohibition on sale of disposable vapor product.
6 (a) A person or entity shall not sell or offer for sale at retail in
7 this state a disposable vapor product from a prohibited foreign party.
8 (b)(1) The Director of Arkansas Tobacco Control may seize and hold for
9 disposition of the courts or the Arkansas Tobacco Control Board any
10 disposable vapor product sold or offered for sale in violation of this
11 section.
12 (2) The processes of seizure, forfeiture, and disposition under
13 § 26-57-247 and the destruction of the seized products under § 26-57-249
14 shall apply to this section.
15 (c) A violation of subsection (a) of this section is a Class A
16 misdemeanor.
17 (d) This section does not apply to a product that has been approved by
18 the United States Food and Drug Administration.
19
20 SECTION 3. DO NOT CODIFY. TEMPORARY LANGUAGE. Notice of act
21 requirements and allowance of a grace period.
22 The Director of Arkansas Tobacco Control shall notify manufacturers,
23 wholesalers, retailers, and vendors licensed under § 26-57-219 of:
24 (1) The requirements of this act and the time frame in which
25 compliance is required; and
26 (2) The allowance of a ninety-day grace period following the
27 effective date of this act during which any disposal vapor product from a
28 prohibited foreign party in their possession shall be liquidated, sold, or
29 disposed of.
