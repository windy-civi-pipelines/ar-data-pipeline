17 TO RECOGNIZE THE ARKANSAS 250 COMMISSION
18 AS IT BEGINS ITS WORK TO CELEBRATE
19 ARKANSAS'S RICH TWO-HUNDRED-FIFTY-YEAR
20 HISTORY FROM THE SIGNING OF THE
21 DECLARATION OF INDEPENDENCE ON JULY 4,
22 1776, THROUGH JULY 4, 2026.
23
24 WHEREAS, on July 4, 2026, the United States of America will celebrate
25 the two hundred fiftieth anniversary of the signing of the Declaration of
26 Independence; and
27
28 WHEREAS, the United States Congress established the United States
29 Semiquincentennial Commission, which is working with America250.org, Inc., to
30 plan and orchestrate this great occasion, and the United States Congress is
31 encouraging states to join and participate in this celebration of the United
32 States of America; and
33
34 WHEREAS, Governor Sarah Huckabee Sanders created the Arkansas 250
35 Commission to plan, organize, and coordinate Arkansas's celebration
36 activities and events leading up to and on July 4, 2026; and

2 WHEREAS, Arkansas and the territory that preceded it have a rich two-
3 hundred-fifty-year history to commemorate; and
4
5 WHEREAS, in 1776, Arkansas was part of the Louisiana Territory and home
6 to Native Americans and early European settlers; and
7
8 WHEREAS, in 1783, a Revolutionary War battle was fought at Arkansas
9 Post; and
10
11 WHEREAS, in 1803, the land that would become Arkansas officially became
12 a part of the United States of America via the acquisition known as the
13 Louisiana Purchase; and
14
15 WHEREAS, Arkansas officially became a territory of the United States of
16 America in 1819 and achieved statehood in 1836; and
17
18 WHEREAS, in the one hundred eighty-nine (189) years since achieving
19 statehood, Arkansas has played a part in the history of our great nation and
20 is rich with stories to tell, legends to recognize, and achievements to
21 celebrate; and
22
23 WHEREAS, this semiquincentennial event is a once-in-a-generation
24 opportunity to engage children, students, and adults in educational
25 activities, civic engagement, and public service opportunities,
26
27 NOW THEREFORE,
28 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
29 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
30
31 THAT the House of Representatives recognize, support, and encourage the
32 Arkansas 250 Commission as it begins its work to celebrate Arkansas's rich
33 two-hundred-fifty-year history from the signing of the Declaration of
34 Independence on July 4, 1776, through July 4, 2026.
35
36 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT the House of Representatives encourage all
2 03/10/2025 11:08:33 AM ADF059

1 educational institutions, civic organizations, and all of Arkansas to join
2 this celebration of our state's magnificent history and achievements in a
3 series of events and activities that will culminate on July 4, 2026.
4
5 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
6 presented to each of the fifteen (15) members of the Arkansas 250 Commission
7 by the Chief Clerk of the House of Representatives.
