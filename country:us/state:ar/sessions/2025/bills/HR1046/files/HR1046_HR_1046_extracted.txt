HOUSE RESOLUTION

9 TO RECOGNIZE THE CONTRIBUTIONS OF HOMESCHOOLING AND
10 TO PROCLAIM FEBRUARY 26, 2025, AS HOMESCHOOL DAY AT
11 THE STATE CAPITOL.
12
13
Subtitle
14
15 TO RECOGNIZE THE CONTRIBUTIONS OF
16 HOMESCHOOLING AND TO PROCLAIM FEBRUARY
17 26, 2025, AS HOMESCHOOL DAY AT THE STATE
18 CAPITOL.
19
20 WHEREAS, over thirty thousand (30,000) students were homeschooled in
21 Arkansas during the 2023-2024 school year; and
22
23 WHEREAS, homeschooling offers flexibility, efficiency, and
24 effectiveness in teaching students in Arkansas, and this benefit allows
25 homeschooling families to offer increased opportunities for community
26 involvement and service to others for their children; and
27
28 WHEREAS, homeschooling is not a one-size-fits-all plan, and the
29 education can be customized to the student's learning styles and preferences
30 and can be beneficial to students with special needs; and
31
32 WHEREAS, homeschooling provides benefits in academics, mental health,
33 physical health, and social development; and
34
35 WHEREAS, homeschooling encourages autonomy and independence and allows
36 children to harness their own unique qualities and interests; and

2 WHEREAS, homeschooling offers opportunities for students to learn
3 entrepreneurship and often to begin working on their own small businesses;
4 and
5
6 WHEREAS, homeschooling emphasizes family as a foundation for social
7 development and development of faith; and
8
9 WHEREAS, homeschooling is accommodating to special situations and works
10 well for military families, families who travel, and families contending with
11 illness and challenging work schedules; and
12
13 WHEREAS, homeschooling parents are able to value an individual
14 student's potential and focus on this individual potential instead of minimum
15 standards and standardized testing, and these parents are allowed to shape
16 homeschooling so that learning for the students can be reactive to new
17 methods and new concepts regarding positive child development; and
18
19 WHEREAS, homeschooling provides for a focus on life skills that
20 students can use as they progress into adulthood as homeschooling allows
21 students to dedicate time to learning anything from cooking to budgeting,
22
23 NOW THEREFORE,
24 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
25 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
26
27 THAT the House of Representatives recognize February 26, 2025, as
28 Homeschool Day at the State Capitol.
