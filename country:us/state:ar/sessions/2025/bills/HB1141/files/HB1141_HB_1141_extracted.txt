21 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code ยง 7-1-103(a), concerning misdemeanors under
22 the laws governing elections in the State of Arkansas, is amended to add an
23 additional subdivision to read as follows:
24 (25)(A)(i) As used in this subdivision (a)(25), "deep fake" means a
25 medium of communication that is deliberately manipulated using artificial
26 intelligence technology to appear to depict a real person performing an
27 action that did not occur for the purpose of attempting to deceive the viewer
28 into believing the action did occur and for which no reasonable observer
29 could believe there was an intent other than to injure the candidate.
30 (ii) As used in this subdivision (a)(25), "deep
31 fake" does not include:
32 (a) A cartoon, caricature, or any other
33 communication that a reasonable viewer would understand to be a satirical
34 representation of the individual;
35 (b) A communication altered only to change the
36 saturation, brightness, contrast, color, or any other superficial quality of
