24 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 23, Chapter 13, Subchapter 2, is
25 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
26 23-13-266. Operating a commercial motor vehicle without sufficient
27 English language proficiency.
28 (a) A person commits the offense of operating a commercial motor
29 vehicle without sufficient English language proficiency if the person:
30 (1) Operates a commercial motor vehicle, as defined under 49
31 C.F.R. 390.5T, as it existed on January 1, 2025, in the state; and
32 (2) Cannot read or speak the English language sufficiently to:
33 (A) Converse with the general public;
34 (B) Understand highway traffic signs and signals in the
35 English language;
36 (C) Respond to official inquiries; and
