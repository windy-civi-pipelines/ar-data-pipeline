26 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 6-81-1604(b), concerning the standards for
27 loan repayments under the State Teacher Education Program, is amended to read
28 as follows:
29 (b) The division shall adopt standards for awarding the loan
30 repayments to a public school teacher with an Arkansas teacher's license an
31 applicant who is eligible to participate in the program under § 6-81-1605
32 teaching in a:
33 (1) Subject area with a teacher shortage; or
34 (2) Geographic area with teacher shortage.
35

1 6-81-1605. Eligibility.
2 To be eligible for the State Teacher Education Program, an applicant
3 shall:
4 (1) Teach full-time at:
5 (A) At a public school district in a subject area or
6 geographic area with a teacher shortage, as identified by the Division of
7 Higher Education in consultation with the Division of Elementary and
8 Secondary Education; or
9 (B) In a public early childhood education program that
10 serves children whose ages may range from birth through prekindergarten;
11 (2) Hold Either:
12 (A) Hold a valid Arkansas teacher's license; or
13 (B) Hold a birth through prekindergarten teaching
14 credential from the Division of Child Care and Early Childhood Education of
15 the Department of Human Services; and
16 (3)(A) Meet additional continuing eligibility criteria
17 established by the Division of Higher Education.
18 (B) An individual who has earned a teaching license upon
19 the successful completion of an alternative educator preparation program is
20 an eligible student under this section.
