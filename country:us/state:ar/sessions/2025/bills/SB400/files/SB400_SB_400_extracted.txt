19 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 2-36-101 is amended to read as follows:
20 2-36-101. Fair-funding program Funding for livestock shows and fairs —
21 Requirements — Districts — Definition.
22 (a) There is created within the Department of Agriculture a fair-
23 funding program that shall be used to provide fair-funding assistance to
24 junior livestock shows, county fairs, district fairs, and the Arkansas State
25 Fair and Livestock Show.
26 (b) The department shall utilize a historical average of total fair
27 funding provided by the State of Arkansas to previous fair-funding recipients
28 in determining an equitable distribution of available funding appropriated by
29 the General Assembly.
30 (c)(1)(A)(1) A recipient of fair funding under the fair-funding
31 program financial assistance provided by the Department of Agriculture under
32 subsection (a) subsection (b) of this section shall:
33 (i) Maintain records of expenditures; and
34 (ii) Submit an annual financial report to the
35 department and to Arkansas Legislative Audit by the end of each calendar
36 year.
