19 IMPROVING THE HEALTH AND WELL-BEING OF
20 CHILDREN AND THEIR FAMILIES AND DIRECTLY
21 IMPACTING THE LOCAL COMMUNITIES OF THE
22 STATE OF ARKANSAS.
23
24 WHEREAS, Ronald McDonald House Charities® has been committed to
25 supporting families when a child is seriously ill since the opening of the
26 first Ronald McDonald House in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, in 1974; and
27
28 WHEREAS, the mission of Ronald McDonald House Charities® to provide
29 essential services that remove barriers, strengthen families, and promote
30 healing when children need healthcare has positively impacted countless
31 families around the world; and
32
33 WHEREAS, Ronald McDonald House Charities® has continued to create new
34 ways to support families along their child's healthcare journey; and
35
36 WHEREAS, since 1974, McDonald's Corporation, its franchisees, and its

6 WHEREAS, Ronald McDonald House Charities® has expanded to more than
7 three hundred eighty-five (385) Ronald McDonald House programs, more than two
8 hundred seventy (270) Ronald McDonald Family Room programs, and more than
9 forty (40) Ronald McDonald Care Mobile programs; and
10
11 WHEREAS, six (6) Ronald McDonald House Charities® programs operate in
12 Arkansas through one (1) Ronald McDonald House Charities® Chapter, providing
13 direct support and comfort to three thousand five hundred eighteen (3,518)
14 children and their families across the State of Arkansas annually; and
15
16 WHEREAS, in 2023 alone, McDonald’s Corporation, along with its Arkansas
17 franchisees and customers, raised more than nine hundred sixty-seven thousand
18 dollars ($967,000) for the local Arkansas Ronald McDonald House Charities®
19 Chapter, which is equivalent to nine thousand six hundred seventy (9,670)
20 overnight stays for families at Ronald McDonald House Charities® programs in
21 Arkansas; and
22
23 WHEREAS, Ronald McDonald House Charities® remains dedicated to
24 enhancing its programs and creating a world where all families have what they
25 need to ensure the best health outcomes for their children,
26
27 NOW THEREFORE,
28 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
29 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
30
31 THAT the House of Representatives recognize Ronald McDonald House
32 Charities® for fifty (50) years of service in improving the health and well-
33 being of children and their families and directly impacting the local
34 communities of the State of Arkansas.
35
