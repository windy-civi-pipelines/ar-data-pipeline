Subtitle
16
17 TO ALLOW ADDITIONAL WAGE LOSS BENEFITS
18 IN ADDITION TO SCHEDULED INJURY PAYMENTS
19 UNDER THE WORKERS' COMPENSATION LAW THAT
20 RESULTED FROM INITIATED MEASURE 1948,
21 NO. 4.
22
23 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
24
25 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 11-9-521(g), concerning an employee
26 suffering a scheduled injury under the Workers' Compensation Law, is amended
27 to read as follows:
28 (g) Any employee suffering a scheduled injury shall not be entitled to
29 permanent partial disability benefits in excess of the percentage of
30 permanent physical impairment set forth above except as otherwise provided in
31 § 11-9-519(b) In considering a claim for permanent partial disability
32 benefits in excess of the employee's percentage of permanent physical
33 impairment, the Workers' Compensation Commission may take into account, in
34 addition to the percentage of permanent physical impairment, such factors as
35 the employee's age, education, work experience, and other matters reasonably
36 expected to affect his or her future earning capacity.
