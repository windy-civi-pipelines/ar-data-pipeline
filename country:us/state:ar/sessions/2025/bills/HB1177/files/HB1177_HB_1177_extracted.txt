22 SECTION 1. DO NOT CODIFY. Legislative findings and intent.
23 (a) The General Assembly finds that:
24 (1) The Supreme Court recognized in Gunn v. Farmers Ins. Exch.,
25 2010 Ark. 434 (2010) and Stockton v. Sentry Ins., 337 Ark. 507 (1999), that:
26 (A) The Arkansas Franchise Practices Act does not apply to
27 the relationship between an insurance company and the insurance company's
28 agents; and
29 (B) A franchise relationship does not exist between an
30 insurance company and the insurance company's agent under the Arkansas
31 Franchise Practices Act;
32 (2) The Supreme Court correctly decided that the Arkansas
33 Franchise Practices Act does not apply to an insurance agent who solicits or
34 sells insurance on behalf of an insurance company;
35 (3) The Supreme Court properly and correctly interpreted and
36 applied the Arkansas Franchise Practices Act; and
