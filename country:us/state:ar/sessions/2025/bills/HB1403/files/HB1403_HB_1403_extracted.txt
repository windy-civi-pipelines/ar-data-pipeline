16 TO AMEND THE ARKANSAS HEALTH AND
17 OPPORTUNITY FOR ME ACT OF 2021 TO ALLOW
18 NONHOSPITAL ENTITIES TO BE COMMUNITY
19 BRIDGE ORGANIZATIONS.
20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
22
23 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 23-61-1002(3), concerning the legislative
24 intent for the Arkansas Health and Opportunity for Me Act of 2021, is amended
25 to read as follows:
26 (3) Promotes the health, welfare, and stability of mothers and
27 their infants after birth through hospital-based community bridge
28 organizations;
29
30 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 23-61-1002(6), concerning the legislative
31 intent for the Arkansas Health and Opportunity for Me Act of 2021, is amended
32 to read as follows:
33 (6) Addresses health-related social needs of Arkansans in rural
34 counties through hospital-based community bridge organizations and reduces
35 the additional risk for disease and premature death associated with living in
36 a rural county;

1
2 SECTION 3. Arkansas Code § 23-61-1010(b)(1), concerning the community
3 bridge organizations under the Arkansas Health and Opportunity for Me Act of
4 2021, is amended to read as follows:
5 (b)(1) Each community bridge organization shall be administered by a
6 hospital or a nonhospital entity under conditions established by the
7 Department of Human Services.
8
9 SECTION 4. Arkansas Code § 23-61-1010(b), concerning the community
10 bridge organizations under the Arkansas Health and Opportunity for Me Act of
11 2021, is amended to add an additional subdivision to read as follows:
12 (5) A nonhospital entity may serve eligible individuals under
13 subsection (a) of this section if the nonhospital entity meets any additional
14 criteria established by the Department of Human Services.
