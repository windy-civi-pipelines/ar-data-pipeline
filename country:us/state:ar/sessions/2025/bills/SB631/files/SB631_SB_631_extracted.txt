26 probation.

32 (A) Time and place of the preliminary hearing;

34 (C) Condition of suspension or probation the defendant is
35 alleged to have violated.

18 is not required if:

20 (2) The revocation is based on the defendant's commission of an

22 criminal proceeding; or
23 (3) The revocation hearing pursuant to subsection (b) of this
24 section is held promptly after the arrest and in the judicial district where
25 the alleged violation occurred or where the defendant was arrested.
