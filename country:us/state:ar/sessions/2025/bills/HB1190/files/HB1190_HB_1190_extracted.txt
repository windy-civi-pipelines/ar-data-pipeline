15 TO CREATE AN INCOME TAX EXEMPTION FOR
16 TEACHERS.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
19
20 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 26, Chapter 51, Subchapter 3, is
21 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
22 26-51-317. Wages from teaching — Definition.
23 (a) As used in this section, "teacher" means a person who is:
24 (1) Engaged in student instruction in kindergarten through grade
25 twelve (K-12) for more than seventy percent (70%) of the person's contracted
26 or employed time; and
27 (2) Employed by a:
28 (A) Public school in a teaching, instructional, or
29 educational capacity that requires a license under § 6-17-401 et seq.; or
30 (B) Open-enrollment charter school in a teaching,
31 instructional, or educational capacity.
32 (b) There is allowed an exemption from the income tax imposed by this
33 chapter for the first fifty thousand dollars ($50,000) of wages received by
34 an individual for employment as a teacher.
35
36 SECTION 2. EFFECTIVE DATE. Section 1 of this act is effective for tax
