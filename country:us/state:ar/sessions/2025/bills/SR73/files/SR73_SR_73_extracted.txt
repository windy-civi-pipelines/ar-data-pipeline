SENATE RESOLUTION
8
10 THE CONSIDERATION OF SJR 6 AS A THIRD PROPOSED
11 CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENT.
12
13
Subtitle
14
15 REQUESTING THAT SENATOR KING BE
16 AUTHORIZED TO PURSUE THE CONSIDERATION
17 OF SJR 6 AS A THIRD PROPOSED
18 CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENT.
19
20 WHEREAS, Section 19(C) of the Joint Rules of the Ninety-Fifth General
21 Assembly provides that a third proposed constitutional amendment shall not be
22 considered or voted upon by the General Assembly until identical resolutions
23 authorizing the consideration of the proposed constitutional amendment have
24 been approved by an affirmative vote of two-thirds (2/3) of the members
25 elected to each house,
26
27 NOW THEREFORE,
28 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE
29 STATE OF ARKANSAS:
30
31 THAT Senator King is authorized to pursue the consideration of SJR 6 as
32 a third proposed constitutional amendment for referral to the electors of the
33 state for approval or rejection.
34
