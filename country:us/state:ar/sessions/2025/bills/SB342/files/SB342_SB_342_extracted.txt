20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:

22 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 20-19-102 is amended to read as follows:
23 20-19-102. Injuries to domesticated animals by dogs — Definition.
24 (a)(1) As used in this section, “domesticated animals” includes, but
25 is not limited to, without limitation sheep, goats, cattle, swine, and
26 poultry, and other livestock.
27 (2) A person owning or having in possession or under control a
28 dog is liable for damages to the owner or owners of a domesticated animal
29 killed or injured by the dog for the full value of the domesticated animal
30 killed or injured.
31 (b)(1) A person engaged in raising domesticated animals or owning
32 domesticated animals who sustains a loss or damage to any of his or her
33 domesticated animals by a dog has a right of action against the owner,
34 possessor, or controller of the dog.
35 (2) A person knowing that a dog has killed or is about to catch,
36 injure, or kill a domesticated animal has the right to kill the dog, without
