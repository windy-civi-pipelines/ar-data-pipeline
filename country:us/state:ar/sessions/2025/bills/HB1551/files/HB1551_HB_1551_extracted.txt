22 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 5, Chapter 61, Subchapter 1, is amended
23 to add an additional section to read as follows:
24 5-61-103. Coerced criminal abortion by means of fraud.
25 (a) As used in this section:
26 (1) "Abortion" means a chemical abortion as defined in ยง 20-16-
27 2502; and
28 (2)(A) "Abortion-inducing drug" means a medicine, drug,
29 combination of drugs or chemicals, or any other substance used with the
30 intent of causing an abortion including without limitation off-label use of
31 drugs known to have abortion-inducing properties, such as mifepristone, RU-
32 486, Mifeprex, Cytotec, misoprostol, and methotrexate.
33 (B) "Abortion-inducing drug" does not include:
34 (i) Drugs that may be known to increase the risk of
35 miscarriage, but which are prescribed for other medical indications such as
36 chemotherapeutic agents or diagnostic drugs; and

2 methotrexate when used to treat an ectopic pregnancy.
3 (b) A person commits coerced criminal abortion by means of fraud if he

6 (c) A person who violates subsection (b) of this section upon
7 conviction is guilty of a Class B felony.
8 (d) A prosecution under this section does not prevent prosecution
9 under another section if a person commits coerced criminal abortion by means
10 of fraud and the use of an abortion-inducing drug causes the death or bodily
11 injury of the pregnant woman or an unborn child.
