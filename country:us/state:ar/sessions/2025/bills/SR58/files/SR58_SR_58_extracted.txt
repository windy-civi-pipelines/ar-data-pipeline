14 TO RECOGNIZE THE ARKADELPHIA HIGH SCHOOL
15 BADGERS FOOTBALL TEAM AS THE 2024 CLASS
16 4A STATE CHAMPIONS.
17
18 WHEREAS, the Arkadelphia High School Badgers football team won the
19 Class 4A state championship held on December 14, 2024, at War Memorial
20 Stadium in Little Rock; and
21
22 WHEREAS, the game was a shutout, with the Badgers earning twenty-eight
23 points while holding the normally high-scoring Elkins Elks to zero points
24 (28-0); and
25
26 WHEREAS, the last shutout prior to this in a state championship
27 football game was in 2018, when the Badgers last won the Class 4A state
28 championship title; and
29
30 WHEREAS, this was the fifth Class 4A state championship win for the
31 Badgers; and
32
33 WHEREAS, the state championship win followed a twenty-two-game
34 conference wining streak for the Badgers, who have not lost to a conference
35 opponent since October 2021; and
36

17 Brumfield, Kaden Marlow, Drake Thevenot, Grant Rollins, Cam Crow, Jacoby

20 WHEREAS, Badgers team managers are Juan Castro, Chase Conroy, Cypress
21 Blakeney, Caiden Dickerson, M'Kayla Morris, Jaret Luna, Cooper Babb, Carson
22 Hood, Layne Wingfield, and Carter Carozza; and

29 touchdowns in a season with forty-six (46); and
30

34

SR58
1
2 WHEREAS, team member Brayden Wagner was named Most Valuable Player of
3 the Class 4A state championship tournament; and
4
5 WHEREAS, the Badgers were led by Head Coach Trey Schucker, Defensive
6 Coordinator and Defensive Back Coach Chris Kizer, Offensive Coordinator and
7 Wide Receiver Coach Benson Jordan, Special Teams and Linebacker Coach Ben
8 Chandler, Defensive Line Coach Bobby Evins, Offensive Line Coach Evan Spears,
9 and Assistant Coaches Brandon Helms and J.T. Williams; and
10
11 WHEREAS, Badgers Head Coach Trey Schucker was voted Class 4A Conference
12 Coach of the Year for the third time, the Hooten's-Farm Bureau Class 4A Coach
13 of the Year, and the KATV Channel 7/Red River Arkansas Football Coach of the
14 Year, and was voted on to the Arkansas Activities Association All-Star
15 Coaching Staff-West Team; and
16
17 WHEREAS, Head Coach Trey Schucker said of the season and the win, "This
18 team represented the Arkadelphia School District and the Arkadelphia
19 community very well with how they played the game of football for each other
20 and showed a true brotherhood. By showing discipline and doing things right
21 on and off the field, this team was able to show what it takes to win a state
22 championship and uphold the winning culture of the Arkadelphia football
23 program",
24
25 NOW THEREFORE,
26 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE
27 STATE OF ARKANSAS:
28
29 THAT the Senate recognize the Arkadelphia High School Badgers football
30 team as the 2024 Class 4A state champions.
31
32 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
33 presented to Head Coach Trey Schucker by the Secretary of the Senate.
