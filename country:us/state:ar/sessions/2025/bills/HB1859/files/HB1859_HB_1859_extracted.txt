19 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 23-99-405 is amended to read as follows:
20 23-99-405. Mastectomies.
21 (a) Every health benefit plan providing mastectomy benefits and issued
22 or renewed after July 16, 2003, shall conform with the requirements of the
23 Women's Health and Cancer Rights Act of 1998, 42 U.S.C. §§ 300gg-6 and 300gg-
24 52, as it existed on January 1, 2003 January 1, 2025.
25 (b) To the extent the requirements of this section do not conflict
26 with federal law, rules, or regulations, each healthcare insurer providing
27 mastectomy benefits in a health benefit plan shall provide, in a manner
28 determined in consultation with the attending physician and the enrollee or
29 insured:
30 (1) For medical and surgical benefits for any hospital stay in
31 connection with a mastectomy for not less than forty-eight (48) hours unless
32 the decision to discharge the patient before the expiration of the minimum
33 length of stay is made by an attending physician in consultation with the
34 enrollee or insured;
35 (2) The following medical and surgical benefits with respect to
36 mastectomy coverage if an enrollee or insured receives benefits in connection
