23 SECTION 1. DO NOT CODIFY. Title.
24 This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Early Childhood
25 Education Workforce Quality Incentive Act".
26
27 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code Title 26, Chapter 51, Subchapter 5, is
28 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
29 26-51-518. Early childhood education workforce quality incentive
30 credit.
31 (a) There is allowed an early childhood education workforce quality
32 incentive credit against the income tax imposed by this chapter in the amount
33 provided under subsection (b) of this section for qualifying early childhood
34 education program directors, teachers, and instructional staff who:
35 (1) Are employed in an early childhood education program that
36 meets the requirements of the Better Beginnings quality rating system of the

5 (b) The income tax credit allowed under this section is allowed in the
6 following amounts:
7 (1) For a taxpayer who has a Child Development Associate

10 (2) For a taxpayer who has an Arkansas Children's Program
11 Administrator Credential recognized by the National Association for the

13 (3) For a taxpayer who has an associate degree in early
14 childhood education or a related field, two thousand four hundred dollars
15 ($2,400); and
16 (4) For a taxpayer who has a bachelor's degree or higher in
17 early childhood education or a related field, three thousand dollars
18 ($3,000).
19 (c) If the amount of the income tax credit allowed under this section
20 exceeds the taxpayer's income tax liability, the excess shall be refunded to
21 the taxpayer.
22 (d) To claim the income tax credit allowed under this section, the
23 taxpayer shall file with his or her individual income tax return an
24 attestation form provided by the Arkansas Professional Development Registry
25 of the Department of Education, verifying that the taxpayer meets the
26 requirements for the income tax credit allowed under this section.
27
28 SECTION 3. EFFECTIVE DATE. Section 2 of this act is effective for tax
29 years beginning on or after January 1, 2025.
