14 TO RECOGNIZE THE MOUNTAIN HOME TENNIS
15 TEAM AS THE CLASS 5A STATE CHAMPIONS.
16
17 WHEREAS, the Mountain Home Bombers boy's tennis team claimed the Class
18 5A state championship in Searcy, Arkansas, on Tuesday, October 15, 2024; and
19
20 WHEREAS, the Bombers were led by Pierce Blackmon and Finley Chafin, who
21 won the boy's Class 5A state doubles title; and
22
23 WHEREAS, Pierce Blackmon and Finley Chafin defeated competitors from
24 the Little Rock Christian and Valley View Blazers teams; and
25
26 WHEREAS, the Bombers were led by Head Coach Skyler King,
27
28 NOW THEREFORE,
29 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
30 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
31
32 THAT the House of Representatives congratulate and recognize the
33 Mountain Home Bombers boys' tennis team as the Class 5A state champions.
34
35 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption of this resolution, a copy be
36 provided to Head Coach Skyler King by the Chief Clerk of the House of
