24 SECTION 1. DO NOT CODIFY. Title.
25 This act shall be known and may be cited as the "Proof of Residency for
26 Public Assistance Act".
27
28 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code Title 20, Chapter 76, Subchapter 1, is
29 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
30 20-76-120. Proof of residency requirement.
31 For an adult applicant for public assistance who claims to reside in
32 this state, the state agency or political subdivision administering the
33 public assistance shall make every reasonable effort to verify the adult
34 applicant's residency, including without limitation requesting the adult
35 applicant present one (1) of the following:
36 (1) A utility bill in the adult applicant's name from at least
