17 20-56-310. Universal registry identification card.
18 (a) An individual who is employed or provides services to a dispensary
19 or cultivation facility shall be issued a universal registry identification
20 card.
21 (b)(1) An individual holding a universal registry identification card
22 shall notify the Alcoholic Beverage Control Division in writing before
23 initiating employment or services to a dispensary or cultivation facility.
24 (2) The division shall maintain a record of each dispensary or
25 cultivation facility that is a designated facility of an individual holding a
26 universal registry identification card.
27 (c) The division may promulgate rules to implement this section.
