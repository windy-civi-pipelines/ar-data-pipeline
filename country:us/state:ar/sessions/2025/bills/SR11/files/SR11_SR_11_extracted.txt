16 TO COMMEMORATE THE 100TH ANNIVERSARY ON
17 FEBRUARY 13, 2025, OF THE ROBERT W. JACK
18 VETERANS OF FOREIGN WARS POST 1322 IN
19 THE CITY OF VAN BUREN, THE OLDEST
20 VETERANS OF FOREIGN WARS POST IN
21 ARKANSAS.
22
23 WHEREAS, veterans in the City of Van Buren and Crawford County,
24 understanding the importance of providing support to veterans, service
25 members, and their families, were granted a charter to organize a Veterans of
26 Foreign Wars Post on February 13, 1925; and
27
28 WHEREAS, the Veterans of Foreign Wars traces its roots to 1899 when
29 veterans of the Spanish-American War and the Philippine Insurrection founded
30 local organizations to secure rights and benefits for their service, as many
31 veterans arrived home from war wounded or sick and found no medical care or
32 veterans' pensions waiting for them; and
33
34 WHEREAS, charter members of the Robert W. Jack Veterans of Foreign Wars
35 Post 1322 representing veterans of the Spanish-American War were Charles
36 Meador, Charles Blevins, and J.P. Brown; and

2 WHEREAS, charter members of the Robert W. Jack Veterans of Foreign Wars
3 Post 1322 representing veterans of World War I, also known as the Great War,
4 were Hugh G. Pope, G.O. Gooding, A. Dewey Lewis, George R. Wood, Robert G.
5 Boatright, Claude L. Sartain, W.A. Baxter, Frank X. Rooney, J.S. Kenny, T.J.
6 Garner, W.E. Lynch, Roy R. Johnson, Roy Franklin, Hugo A. Seelbinder, Harry
7 Line, Phillip Cornelius, and Allen Taylor; and
8
9 WHEREAS, the charter members of the Robert W. Jack Veterans of Foreign
10 Wars Post 1322 unanimously chose to honor the memory of Robert W. Jack
11 because Jack gave his life on September 22, 1918, during the waning hours of
12 the Battle of Saint-Miheil in France, and was a good friend to charter member
13 Hugh G. Pope; and
14
15 WHEREAS, the Robert W. Jack Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 1322 rapidly
16 became a beacon of hope and support for veterans that served in the Spanish-
17 American War and World War I; and
18
19 WHEREAS, for the past one hundred (100) years, the dedication of the
20 charter members of the Robert W. Jack Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 1322 has
21 created more than a building or organization for veterans; it is a place
22 veterans can call home and a place that provides essential services to men
23 and women who have served the nation in the United States Armed Forces; and
24
25 WHEREAS, the Robert W. Jack Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 1322 predated
26 the establishment of the Department of Arkansas, Veterans of Foreign Wars of
27 the United States and is currently the oldest Veterans of Foreign Wars post
28 in Arkansas; and
29
30 WHEREAS, in 2019, the Robert W. Jack Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 1322
31 began work on Vision 2025, a project encompassing construction of a facility
32 that will embody a comprehensive approach to health and support for veterans;
33 and
34
35 WHEREAS, Vision 2025 will include a mental health clinic, a physical
36 therapy clinic, and a physician's clinic as well as a Federal Emergency

SR11
1 Management Agency-certified storm shelter, a community room, a historical
2 museum, and an outdoor walking trail; and
3
4 WHEREAS, the mission of the Robert Jack Veterans of Foreign Wars Post
5 1322 is to serve the veterans, citizens, and youth of the City of Van Buren
6 and surrounding communities with existing programs such as OPERATION: Daily
7 Battle, Hiking for Mental Health, Gaming Against PTSD, Voices of Democracy
8 and Patriot's Pen youth essay contests, helping food-insecure veterans and
9 their families with food and assistance with bills, and providing wheelchair-
10 accessible ramps; and
11
12 WHEREAS, the Robert W. Jack Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 1322
13 recognizes the ongoing and changing needs of its veterans and adapts the
14 services it provides to the needs of the veterans of the City of Van Buren
15 and Crawford County; and
16
17 WHEREAS, the Robert W. Jack Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 1322 serves
18 as a model for other Veterans of Foreign Wars posts throughout Arkansas and
19 the nation,
20
21 NOW THEREFORE,
22 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE
23 STATE OF ARKANSAS:
24
25 THAT the Senate commemorate and celebrate the one hundredth anniversary
26 of the Robert W. Jack Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 1322 in the City of Van
27 Buren.
28
29 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
30 provided to Robert W. Jack Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 1322 Commander
31 Matthew Hicks by the Secretary of the Senate.
