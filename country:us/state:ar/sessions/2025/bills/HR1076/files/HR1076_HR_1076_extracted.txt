21 WHEREAS, the Honorable Stacey Zimmerman, the longest-serving juvenile
22 judge serving Madison County and Washington County, Arkansas, has retired
23 after thirty-four (34) years of service; and
24
25 WHEREAS, the Honorable Stacey Zimmerman earned her Bachelor of Science
26 degree in Agricultural Economics from Texas Tech University in 1983,
27 graduating magna cum laude; and
28

31 Texas Tech University in 1986; and
32

34 in Agricultural Law from the University of Arkansas at Fayetteville School of
35 Law in 1988; and

1 WHEREAS, the Honorable Stacey Zimmerman maintained a private law
2 practice in the City of Fayetteville from 1989, and was Deputy Prosecutor
3 with the Washington County Juvenile Court from 1993-1998; and
4
5 WHEREAS, the Honorable Stacey Zimmerman was appointed by then-Governor
6 Michael Dale "Mike" Huckabee to the Criminal Detention Facility Review
7 Committee and served as Special Judge for Benton County Juvenile Court; and
8
9 WHEREAS, the Honorable Stacey Zimmerman has served as volunteer
10 attorney ad litem for children in Madison County and Washington County; and
11
12 WHEREAS, the Honorable Stacey Zimmerman was elected as Circuit/Chancery
13 Judge, Juvenile Division, Fourth Judicial Circuit, Madison County and
14 Washington County, in November 1998; and
15
16 WHEREAS, the Honorable Stacey Zimmerman was named one of the Forty
17 Under Forty by Northwest Arkansas Business Journal in 1999 and is a member of
18 the Arkansas Bar Association and the State Bar of Texas as well as the Rotary
19 Club of Fayetteville; and
20
21 WHEREAS, the Honorable Stacey Zimmerman has served with the Arkansas
22 Bar Association's Juvenile Law Committee, is a past secretary/treasurer of
23 the Washington County Bar Association, is a past member of the Washington
24 County Juvenile Justice Advisory Board, and has participated in the National
25 Conference on Juvenile Justice; and
26
27 WHEREAS, the Honorable Stacey Zimmerman is married to Calvin W. Farley,
28 Vice President of Farley's, Incorporated of Siloam Springs, and they have one
29 (1) son, Colton,
30

32 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL

34
35 THAT the House of Representatives recognize the Honorable Stacey
36 Zimmerman upon the occasion of her retirement for her service to the State of
