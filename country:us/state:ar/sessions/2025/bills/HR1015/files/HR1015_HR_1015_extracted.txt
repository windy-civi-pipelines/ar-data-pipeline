14 TO RECOGNIZE THE MOUNTAIN HOME CROSS
15 COUNTRY TEAM AS THE CLASS 5A STATE
16 CHAMPIONS.
17
18 WHEREAS, the Mountain Home Bombers boy's cross country team claimed the
19 Class 5A state championship at Oaklawn in Hot Springs, Arkansas, on Thursday,
20 November 7, 2024; and
21
22 WHEREAS, this was the third consecutive and seventh overall Class 5A
23 state championship win for the Bombers; and
24
25 WHEREAS, the Bombers scored fifty-seven (57) points more than the
26 runners-up, the Hot Springs Lakeside Rams; and
27
28 WHEREAS, Jackson Free won the individual title with a time of sixteen
29 hours, six minutes, and seventeen seconds (16:06:17), and was one of five
30 runners for the Bombers who made the All-State team; and
31
32 WHEREAS, of the four (4) other runners for the Bombers who made the
33 All-State team, Conner King took third place, Garrett Barr took fourth place,
34 Trenton Smith took sixth place, and Wave Simmons took nineteenth place at the
35 state championship; and
36

1 WHEREAS, the Bomber boy's cross country team was led by coaches Wil
2 Norris, Tilda Helgesson, and Roddy Patrick,
3
4 NOW THEREFORE,
5 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
6 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
7
8 THAT the House of Representatives congratulate and recognize the
9 Mountain Home Bombers boys' cross country team as the Class 5A state
10 champions.
11
12 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption of this resolution, a copy be
13 provided to Coaches Wil Norris, Tilda Helgesson, and Roddy Patrick by the
14 Chief Clerk of the House of Representatives.
