20 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 14-42-103 is amended to read as follows:
21 14-42-103. Vacancies in municipal offices generally.
22 (a)(1) Vacancies in municipal offices that are authorized by state law
23 to be filled by appointment by the city or town governing body require a
24 majority vote of the remaining members of the governing body.
25 (2) However, a majority of a quorum of the whole number of the
26 governing body is required to fill the vacancy.

28 members of a governing body, to fill the vacancy.
29 (2) However, a member of the governing body shall not vote on
30 his or her own appointment.
31 (c) This section does not apply to circumstances prescribed under §
32 14-43-501(a) or § 14-43-411(a) A vacancy of a municipal office in a
33 municipality, regardless of the classification or form of government of the
34 municipality, shall be filled according to one (1) of the following methods:
35 (1)(A) At the first council meeting after the declaration of the
36 vacancy, the governing body of the municipality shall elect by a majority
