Subtitle
14
15 TO DESIGNATE MARCH 12, 2025, AS STARS OF
16 LIFE DAY AND TO RECOGNIZE THE
17 ACCOMPLISHMENTS OF CERTAIN EMERGENCY
18 MEDICAL SERVICES PROFESSIONALS.
19
20 WHEREAS, emergency medical services are a vital public service; and
21
22 WHEREAS, the members of emergency medical services teams are ready to
23 provide lifesaving care to those in need twenty-four (24) hours a day, seven
24 (7) days a week; and
25
26 WHEREAS, the members of emergency medical services teams, whether
27 career or volunteer, often go beyond the call of duty to carry out lifesaving
28 measures and to promote the causes of the ambulance industry; and
29
30 WHEREAS, it is appropriate to recognize the value and the
31 accomplishments of selected individuals who epitomize the spirit of emergency
32 medical services,
33
34 NOW THEREFORE,
35 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL
36 ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
*ADF139*
03/06/2025 9:56:58 AM ADF139
HR1075
1
2 THAT the House of Representatives proclaim March 12, 2025, as Stars of
3 Life Day and, in honor thereof, recognize the individuals presented as
4 Arkansas EMS Stars of Life.
5
6 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
7 presented to each Arkansas EMS Star of Life by the Chief Clerk of the House
8 of Representatives.
