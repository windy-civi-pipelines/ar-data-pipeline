5 15-3-704. Administration.
6 (a)(1) The Arkansas Small Business Innovation Research Matching Grant
7 Program is created.
8 (2) The Division of Science and Technology of the Arkansas
9 Economic Development Commission shall administer the program.
10 (b) The division commission shall:
11 (1) Create application forms to be submitted by eligible
12 businesses seeking a matching grant from the program;
13 (2) Devise an application process that:
14 (A) Defines the eligibility criteria for a matching grant;
15 and
16 (B) Establishes application submittal and review
17 processes;
18 (3) Define a process by which matching grants may be awarded;
19 and
20 (4) Execute standard legal grant agreements and other
21 documentation governing the disbursement and use of matching grants.
22
23 15-3-705. Eligibility.
24 (a) To be eligible for a matching grant under this subchapter, an
25 applicant shall:
26 (1) Be an eligible business; and
27 (2) Certify that:
28 (A) The eligible business:
29 (i) For Phase I applications, has received a Small
30 Business Innovation Research grant or a Small Business Technology Transfer
31 grant from a sponsoring agency in response to a specific federal
32 solicitation; or
33 (ii) For Phase II applications, has:
34 (a) Submitted a final Phase I report to the
35 sponsoring agency;
36 (b) Demonstrated that the sponsoring agency
2 02/11/2025 4:43:03 PM JLL188
SB218
1 has interest in the Phase II proposal; and
2 (c) Submitted a Phase II proposal to the
3 sponsoring agency; and
4 (B) All cognizant federal Small Business Innovation
5 Research grant requirements will be met.
6 (b) An eligible business awarded a matching grant under this
7 subchapter shall:
8 (1) Remain in Arkansas for the duration of the matching grant
9 project award period;
10 (2) Designate an Arkansas resident or employee as the principal
11 investigator/project manager during the duration of the matching grant award
12 period; and
13 (3) Be principally engaged in one (1) or more of the following
14 targeted business activities:
15 (A) Advanced materials and manufacturing systems;
16 (B) Agriculture, food, and environmental sciences;
17 (C) Biotechnology, bioengineering, and life sciences;
18 (D) Information technology;
19 (E) Transportation logistics; and
20 (F) Bio-based products.
21
22 15-3-706. Application requirements.
23 (a) To request a matching grant under this subchapter, an applicant
24 shall complete and submit the application forms prescribed by the Division of
25 Science and Technology of the Arkansas Economic Development Commission under
26 § 15-3-704.
27 (b) An applicant shall submit an application for each federal Phase I
28 and federal Phase II grant proposal for which the applicant is requesting a
29 matching grant.
30 (c) The division commission shall:
31 (1) Review applications:
32 (A) In order of receipt, as determined by the date and
33 time stamp of receipt; and
34 (B) In accordance with rules promulgated by the division
35 commission under § 15-3-709; and
36 (2) Not consider an incomplete or noncompliant application and

1 shall return an incomplete or a noncompliant application without further
2 review;
3 (3) Review each application with the advice and recommendation
4 of the Commercialization Committee of the Board of Directors of the Division
5 of Science and Technology of the Arkansas Economic Development Commission;
6 and
7 (4) Provide advice to the Director of the Arkansas Economic
8 Development Commission concerning the applications for matching grants
9 reviewed by the division.
10 (d)(1) Applications submitted to the division commission are subject
11 to the Freedom of Information Act of 1967, § 25-19-101 et seq.
12 (2) To the extent an applicant believes that information in an
13 application is confidential or otherwise exempt under the Freedom of
14 Information Act of 1967, § 25-19-101 et seq., the applicant shall
15 specifically designate in writing the information the applicant believes to
16 be confidential or exempt and the basis for the confidentiality or exemption
17 on that portion of the application in which the information appears.
18
19 15-3-707. Matching grant awards.
20 (a) A matching grant awarded under this subchapter:
21 (1) Shall not:
22 (A) Be awarded under this subchapter unless offered in
23 writing by the Director of the Arkansas Economic Development Commission; and
24 (B) Exceed fifty percent (50%) of the federal Small
25 Business Innovation Research award or the Small Business Technology Transfer
26 award, up to:
27 (i) Fifty thousand dollars ($50,000) for a matching
28 grant awarded to match a federal Phase I award; and
29 (ii) One hundred thousand dollars ($100,000) for a
30 matching grant awarded to match a federal Phase II award; and
31 (2) Subject to funding and the discretion of the director, may
32 be offered to an eligible applicant that successfully completes the
33 application process.
34 (b) The matching grant agreement between the Division of Science and
35 Technology of the Arkansas Economic Development Commission and the eligible
36 applicant shall delineate all requirements of the matching grant.
4 02/11/2025 4:43:03 PM JLL188
SB218
1 (c) At least fifty-one percent (51%) of the amount awarded for the
2 matching grant shall be spent in Arkansas.
3 (d) Disbursements for matching grants shall be made on a reimbursable
4 basis, payable when invoices and financial reports are submitted to and
5 approved by the division commission.
6 (e) An eligible business shall not receive more than three (3)
7 matching grants under this subchapter.
8
9 SECTION 4. Arkansas Code § 15-3-709 is amended to read as follows:
10 15-3-709. Rules.
11 The Division of Science and Technology of the Arkansas Economic
12 Development Commission shall promulgate rules to carry out the purposes of
13 this subchapter.
