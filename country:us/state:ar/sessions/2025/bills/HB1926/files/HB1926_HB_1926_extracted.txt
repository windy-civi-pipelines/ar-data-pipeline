18 TO AMEND THE LAW CONCERNING THE
19 SUSPENSION, REVOCATION, OR NONRENEWAL OF
20 A PERSON'S DRIVER'S LICENSE FOR FAILURE

22 AMEND THE LAW CONCERNING A DEFENDANT'S

25 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
26
27 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 16-13-702(a)(5)(A), concerning the
28 immediate payment of a court imposed fine by a defendant, is amended to read
29 as follows:
30 (5)(A)(i) If the defendant claims an inability to pay the fine,
31 the court shall inquire into the defendant's ability to pay and shall make a
32 determination of the defendant's financial ability to pay the fine.
33 (ii) To assist the court in making a determination
34 of the defendant's financial ability to pay a fine under subdivision
35 (a)(5)(A)(i) of this section, the defendant shall provide to the court
36 information regarding the defendant's:

8 (h) Other necessary monthly costs.

10 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 16-13-708(a), concerning the revocation,
11 suspension, or nonrenewal of a driver's license requested by a court from the

13 subdivision read as follows:
14 (4) When making a determination to request a revocation,
15 suspension, or nonrenewal of a person's driver's license under subdivision
16 (a)(1) of this section, the court shall consider the information provided
17 under § 16-13-702(a)(5)(A)(ii).
18
19 SECTION 3. Arkansas Code § 16-13-708(b), concerning the revocation,
20 suspension, or nonrenewal of a driver's license requested by a court from the
21 Department of Finance and Administration, is amended to read as follows:
22 (b)(1) For a driver's license revocation, suspension, or nonrenewal,
23 the court must provide the department with the reason for the revocation,
24 suspension, or nonrenewal, the amount the person owes the court, and the
25 person's full name, date of birth, and last known address.
26 (2) If a person fails to provide the court with the information
27 required under § 16-13-702(a)(5)(A)(ii), the court shall include a
28 notification to the department of the person's failure to provide the
29 required information with the court's request for a driver's license
30 revocation, suspension, or nonrenewal.
