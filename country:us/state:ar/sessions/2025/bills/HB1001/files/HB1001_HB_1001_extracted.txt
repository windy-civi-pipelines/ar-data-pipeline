3 SECTION 2. SPECIAL LANGUAGE. NOT TO BE INCORPORATED INTO THE ARKANSAS
4 CODE NOR PUBLISHED SEPARATELY AS SPECIAL, LOCAL AND TEMPORARY LAW.
5 TRANSFERS. If the Speaker of the House and the Chief of Staff or his
6 designee determine that any item or portion thereof appropriated herein for a
7 specific purpose is not needed for such purposes, they may certify such fact
8 to the State Auditor, and the State Auditor shall cause transfer to be made
9 from one item to another between any appropriations authorized by the General
10 Assembly for the House of Representatives.
11
12 SECTION 3. SPECIAL LANGUAGE. NOT TO BE INCORPORATED INTO THE ARKANSAS
13 CODE NOR PUBLISHED SEPARATELY AS SPECIAL, LOCAL AND TEMPORARY LAW. USES OF
14 APPROPRIATION. The funds appropriated in this Act shall be used to defray
15 the expenses of the House of Representatives of the Legislative Session of
16 the Ninety-Fourth Ninety-Fifth General Assembly, and upon adjournment of the
17 Legislative Session, any unexpended balance of the funds appropriated in this
18 Act may be used to defray the necessary expenses of the operation of the
19 Office of the Speaker of the House of Representatives, the Office of the
20 Chief of Staff, the Office of the Coordinator of House Legislative Services,
21 the Office of Parliamentarian, the Chief Clerk's Office, House Member
22 Services Office and other reasonable and necessary expenses of the House of
23 Representatives of the Ninety-Fourth Ninety-Fifth General Assembly incurred
24 during the interim between the adjournment of the Legislative Session of the
25 Ninety-Fourth Ninety-Fifth and July 1, 2024 2025.
26 Additionally, if it is determined by the Speaker of the House that the
27 presence, assistance, oversight and expertise of any House member is needed
28 in the interim between the legislative sessions, and if it is determined by
29 the Speaker of the House that certain expenses and per diem of members of the
30 House should be paid by the House, such expenses and per diem at the rate
31 authorized for attending legislative sessions shall be paid from the funds
32 appropriated herein for Maintenance, Operations and Expenses.
33
34 SECTION 4. SPECIAL LANGUAGE. NOT TO BE INCORPORATED INTO THE ARKANSAS
35 CODE NOR PUBLISHED SEPARATELY AS SPECIAL, LOCAL AND TEMPORARY LAW. SALARY
36 LEVELS. The Speaker of the House of Representatives and the Chief of Staff
2 01-15-2025 09:42:14 JAR018
As Engrossed: H1/15/25 HB1001
1 or his designee shall, during the month of June, each fiscal year, meet and
2 determine the actual salaries to be paid each employee beginning the
3 following July 1. Such salaries of the employees will be based upon an
4 evaluation of the performance of the Chief of Staff by the Speaker of the
5 House of Representatives and upon an evaluation of the performance of the
6 other employees by the Chief of Staff or his designee.
7
8 SECTION 5. EMERGENCY CLAUSE. It is found and determined by the
9 General Assembly, that the House of Representatives of the 95th General
10 Assembly convenes in Regular Session on January 13, 2025; that expenses of
11 the session begin immediately; and that the expenses are a just expense of
12 the state which must be paid at the time the services are provided.
13 Therefore, an emergency is hereby declared to exist and this Act being
14 necessary for the immediate preservation of the public peace, health and
15 safety shall be in full force and effect from and after the date of its
16 passage and approval. If the bill is neither approved nor vetoed by the
17 Governor, it shall become effective on the expiration of the period of time
18 during which the Governor may veto the bill. If the bill is vetoed by the
19 Governor and the veto is overridden, it shall become effective on the date
20 the last house overrides the veto.
