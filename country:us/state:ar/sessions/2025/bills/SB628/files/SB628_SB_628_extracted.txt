22 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 16-13-506 is amended to read as follows:
23 16-13-506. Court reporters — Transcript fees.
24 (a)(1) When required to make a transcript of court proceedings, each
25 court reporter of the circuit courts shall be entitled to compensation at the
26 rate of four dollars and ten cents ($4.10) eight dollars ($8.00) per page for
27 the original and two (2) copies and at the rate of fifty cents (50¢) one
28 dollar ($1.00) per page for each additional copy.
29 (2) When required to prepare photocopied evidence as part of a
30 transcript, each court reporter shall be entitled to compensation at the rate
31 of one dollar and fifty cents ($1.50) two dollars and fifty cents ($2.50) per
32 page for an original and two (2) copies and at the rate of fifty cents (50¢)
33 one dollar ($1.00) per page for each additional copy thereafter, with the
34 cost to be paid by the parties ordering transcripts.
35 (3) The circuit court judge in which the court proceeding
36 occurred is exempt from this section The official court reporter for a

17 (a) of this section shall be paid by the State of Arkansas.

19 shall be entitled to compensation from the state only for the original and
20 two (2) copies of the transcript.
21 (C) If additional persons request copies of the
22 transcript, the court reporter shall be entitled to payment from the persons
23 requesting the copies at the rate specified in subsection (a) of this
24 section.
25 (2) The payments from the state shall be made only upon
26 certification of the payments by the presiding circuit judge and shall be
27 paid by the Administrative Office of the Courts from funds appropriated out
28 of the Court Reporter's Fund.
29 (c) When the state is required to pay the court reporter for the cost
30 of the preparation of a transcript, § 16-13-510 applies and the state shall
31 comply with a court reporter's request for the payment of a deposit under §
32 16-13-510(c)(1).
