*ANS079*
12/13/2024 2:09:25 PM ANS079
SB10
1 The Arkansas Natural Resources Commission:
2 (1) Shall monitor the impact of blockchain networks and digital
3 asset mining on water usage in this state;
4 (2) May take action under § 15-22-204 against a blockchain
5 network or a digital asset mining business if the impact of the blockchain
6 network or digital asset mining business threatens the critical groundwater
7 supplies of this state through an excessive use of water; and
8 (3)(A) Shall promulgate rules to implement this section.
9 (B) A proposed rule by the commission promulgated under
10 this section shall be approved by the Legislative Council or Joint Budget
11 Committee under § 10-3-309.
12
13 14-1-608. Arkansas Public Service Commission to monitor impact of
14 blockchain networks and digital asset mining on electric grid — Rules.
15 The Arkansas Public Service Commission:
16 (1) Shall monitor the impact of blockchain networks and digital
17 asset mining on the electric grid;
18 (2) May direct a utility to halt service to a blockchain network
19 or digital asset mining business if the impact of the blockchain network or
20 digital asset mining business threatens the reliability of the electric grid;
21 and
22 (3)(A) Shall promulgate rules to implement this section.
23 (B) A proposed rule by the commission promulgated under
24 this section shall be approved by the Legislative Council or Joint Budget
25 Committee under § 10-3-309.
