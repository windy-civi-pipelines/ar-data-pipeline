2 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 27-35-210(p)(1)—(3), concerning the
3 issuance of a permit for special cargo issued by the State Highway Commission
4 to transport farm machinery equipment, is amended to read as follows:
5 (p)(1) Except as provided in subdivision (a)(2)(A) of this section,
6 the commission may issue a special permit valid for one (1) year authorizing
7 the movement of a vehicle hauling farm machinery equipment that exceeds the
8 maximum width authorized under § 27-35-206, but does not exceed twelve feet
9 (12′), if a farm machinery equipment dealer hauler:
10 (A) Applies to the commission for the special permit; and
11 (B) Pays a fee not to exceed five hundred dollars ($500)
12 per vehicle authorized under this subdivision (p)(1).
13 (2) A farm machinery equipment dealer hauler is responsible for
14 the safe routing of a vehicle issued a permit under subdivision (p)(1) of
15 this section, including without limitation ensuring the highways traveled by
16 the vehicle are sufficiently wide for the safety of the vehicle and the
17 traveling public.
18 (3) The commission may require that a farm machinery equipment
19 dealer hauler provide a bond or other security to compensate the Arkansas
20 Department of Transportation in the event of:
21 (A) Damage to a highway or a highway structure caused by a
22 vehicle issued a permit under subdivision (p)(1) of this section; or
23 (B) Costs related to the extrication of a vehicle issued a
24 permit under subdivision (p)(1) of this section from a width-restricted
25 highway or a highway construction or maintenance zone.
