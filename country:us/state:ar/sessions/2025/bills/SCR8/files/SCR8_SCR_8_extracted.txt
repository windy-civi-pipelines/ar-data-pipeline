23 WHEREAS, the Mills University Studies High School Lady Comets
24 basketball team won the Class 4A conference championship and made it to the
25 semifinals of the Class 4A state championship tournament, pressing through
26 and finishing strong for Head Coach Joe Gregory after the tragic death of his
27 wife, Cynthia Gregory, in January 2025; and
28
29 WHEREAS, the 2024-2025 season of the Lady Comets basketball team ended
30 with a loss to De Queen in the semifinals of the Class 4A state championship
31 tournament, but the Lady Comets endured and accomplished so much during this
32 extraordinary season and emerged with a number of school records; and
33
34 WHEREAS, for the Lady Comets, this was the first twenty-win season in
35 eighteen (18) years, the first conference championship in eighteen (18)
36 years, the first regional championship in program history, and the first

28
29 THAT the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring therein,
30 recognize the Mills University Studies High School Lady Comets basketball
31 team as the Class 4A conference champions and remember Head Coach Joe
32 Gregory's wife, Cynthia Gregory.
33
34 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
35 presented to Head Coach Joe Gregory by the Secretary of the Senate.
