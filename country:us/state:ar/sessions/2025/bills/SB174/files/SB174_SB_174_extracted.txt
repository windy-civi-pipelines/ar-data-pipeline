15 TO MAKE TECHNICAL CORRECTIONS TO TITLE 8
16 OF THE ARKANSAS CODE CONCERNING
17 ENVIRONMENTAL LAW.
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
20
21 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 8-9-410(d), concerning used tire programs,
22 is reenacted to ratify the decision by the Arkansas Code Revision Commission
23 to insert “may” in order to correct a grammatical error.
24 (d) The tire accountability board in each of the used tire programs
25 may enter into an interlocal agreement to determine the highest level of
26 efficiency regarding tire processing in the respective used tire program.
27
28 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 8-9-802(4)(A), concerning legislative
29 findings under the Arkansas Nuclear Recycling Program, is amended to read as
30 follows to clarify a reference:
31 (A) University of Arkansas system, in conjunction with
32 other institutions of higher education, can and is willing to provide a
33 detailed analysis examining the benefits of “New Nuclear” compared to the
34 risks of continued storage of spent nuclear fuel at Arkansas Nuclear One;
35
36 SECTION 3. DO NOT CODIFY. CONSTRUCTION AND LEGISLATIVE INTENT.

SB174
1 It is the intent of the General Assembly that:
2 (1) The enactment and adoption of this act shall not expressly
3 or impliedly repeal an act passed during the regular session of the Ninety-
4 Fifth General Assembly;
5 (2) To the extent that a conflict exists between an act of the
6 regular session of the Ninety-Fifth General Assembly and this act:
7 (A) The act of the regular session of the Ninety-Fifth
8 General Assembly shall be treated as a subsequent act passed by the General
9 Assembly for the purposes of:
10 (i) Giving the act of the regular session of the
11 Ninety-Fifth General Assembly its full force and effect; and
12 (ii) Amending or repealing the appropriate parts of
13 the Arkansas Code of 1987; and
14 (B) Section 1-2-107 shall not apply; and
15 (3) This act shall make only technical, not substantive, changes
16 to the Arkansas Code of 1987.
