23 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 10-2-223 is repealed.
24 10-2-223. Authorization to meet during legislative recess —
25 Compensation.
26 (a) The various committees of the General Assembly that are authorized
27 to meet during the interim between legislative sessions are hereby authorized
28 to meet during a recess of the General Assembly of two (2) weeks or more in
29 duration.
30 (b) A member of the General Assembly attending meetings of his or her
31 assigned committees or meetings of committees to which he or she is invited
32 by the chair or chairs during the recess shall be entitled to mileage and per
33 diem at the same rate and from the same sources as for attending committee
34 meetings held after sine die adjournment of the General Assembly.
