1 (a) Except as provided in subsections (b) and (d) of this section, the
2 following are not subject to civil liability for damages, injury, or death
3 resulting from or arising out of a negligent act or omission by a volunteer
4 security person who is providing safety, security, or protection services for
5 a church or other place of worship:
6 (1) The volunteer security person providing the safety,
7 security, or protection services; and
8 (2) The church or other place of worship for which the volunteer
9 security person is providing safety, security, or protection services.
10 (b) A church or other place of worship or a volunteer security person
11 is entitled to civil immunity under this section if the:
12 (1) Church or other place of worship requires adequate training
13 in the provision of safety, security, or protection services for a person to
14 become a volunteer security person; and
15 (2) Volunteer security person has received the training required
16 by the church or other place of worship.
17 (c) The civil immunity provided by this section includes without
18 limitation civil immunity for the use of nonlethal or lethal force in
19 providing safety, security, or protection services.
20 (d) The civil immunity provided by this section does not apply to or
21 include an act or omission that arises out of intentional, reckless, or
22 grossly negligent conduct.
