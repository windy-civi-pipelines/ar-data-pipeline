18 TO REQUIRE A FINANCIAL INSTITUTION TO
19 RENEW CERTAIN CERTIFICATES OF DEPOSIT AT
20 THE HIGHEST INTEREST RATE CLOSEST TO THE
21 TERM OF THE PREVIOUS CERTIFICATE OF
22 DEPOSIT AND FOR THE SAME TERM.
23
24 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
25
26 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 23, Chapter 32, Subchapter 2, is
27 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
28 23-32-211. Certificates of deposit — Renewal at highest interest rate.
29 If a financial institution does not have instructions from a
30 certificate holder concerning a renewal of a certificate of deposit, the
31 financial institution shall renew the certificate of deposit:
32 (1) At the highest fixed interest rate that is closest to the
33 fixed interest rate of the term or period of time of the previous certificate
34 of deposit; and
35 (2) For the same term or period of time as the previous
36 certificate of deposit.
