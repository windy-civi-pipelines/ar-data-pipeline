"Shared Services for mapping and land surveying tasks;

contractor, or other person acting on behalf of the Arkansas Department of
Transportation, State Highway Commission, Arkansas Highway Police Division of
the Arkansas Department of Transportation, or a city or county department of
public works;
(22) If the image is captured by an employee of an insurance
company or an affiliate of an insurance company in connection with the
underwriting of an insurance policy, or the rating or adjusting of an
insurance claim, regarding real property or a structure on real property;
(23) By an engineer or surveyor licensed in the State of
Arkansas for the purpose of:
(A) Mapping and land surveying tasks;
(B) Collecting geographical information system data
supporting public or private infrastructure; or
(24) For the operation by or on behalf of an owner or operator
of a critical infrastructure facility for the purpose of siting, deploying,
inspecting, monitoring, operating, or maintaining the facility."
