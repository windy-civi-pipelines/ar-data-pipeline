21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
22
23 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 7-5-310(b)(2)(B), concerning who may assist
24 voters with disabilities at the polling machine, is amended to read as
25 follows:
26 (B) A person named by the voter who:
27 (i) Is present in the polling site;
28 (ii) 1s eighteen (18) years of age or older; and
29 (iii) Presents a document or identification card
30 that meets the requirements established by the State Board of Election
31 Commissioners.
32
33 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 7-5-310(b)(5), concerning assisting voters
34 with disabilities, is amended to read as follows:
35 (5)(A) It shall be the duty of the poll workers at the polling
36 site to make and maintain a list of the names and addresses of all persons
