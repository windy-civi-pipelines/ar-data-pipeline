(3) "Solicit" means the initiation of in-person communication,

communication, internet direct message communication, or any other electronic
direct message communication, or any combination of communications, by a
chiropractic physician, including by his or her employee, agent, independent
contractor, or procurer.

this section shall recover:
(i) The pecuniary loss caused by the violation;
(ii) Court costs;
(iii) Reasonable attorney's fee; and
(iv) The greater of:
(a) Five hundred dollars ($500); or
(b) Twice the amount of the pecuniary loss.
