16 TO AMEND THE DOMESTIC ABUSE ACT OF 1991;
17 AND TO CLARIFY THE REQUIREMENTS FOR
18 DISMISSAL FOR A LACK OF JURISDICTION
19 OVER A PETITION FOR AN ORDER OF
20 PROTECTION.
21
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
23
24 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 9-15-204(a), concerning a hearing held on a
25 petition for an order of protection, is amended to add an additional
26 subdivision to read as follows:
27 (3) If the court lacks jurisdiction to rule on a petition under
28 § 9-15-201, the court may dismiss a petition for an order of protection
29 through a written order that specifies the basis for the lack of
30 jurisdiction.
