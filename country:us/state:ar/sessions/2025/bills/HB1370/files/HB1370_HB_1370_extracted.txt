26 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 6-10-127 is amended to read as follows:
27 6-10-127. Making up missed school days.
28 (a) If a superintendent closes school for a regularly scheduled school
29 day due to an exceptional or emergency circumstances such as a contagious
30 disease outbreak, inclement weather, or other acts of God circumstance, then
31 the superintendent may make up missed school days by adding time to the
32 beginning or ending end of a regular school day for a minimum of sixty (60)
33 minutes.
34 (b)(1) Each public school district shall include an additional seven
35 (7) days or the hourly equivalent of seven (7) days, as applicable, in the
36 public school district's annual school calendar.

20 (c)(1) To Except as provided under subdivision (c)(2) of this section,
21 to be eligible for funds to implement the salary increases under subsections
22 (a) and (b) of this section, each public school district and open-enrollment
23 public charter school shall be open for on-site, in-person instruction for at
24 least:
25 (1)(A) One hundred seventy-eight (178) days; or
26 (2)(B) One thousand sixty-eight (1,068) hours.

30 or emergency circumstance shall count towards the required number of days or
31 hours, whichever the public school district uses, as required under
32 subdivision (c)(1) of this section.
