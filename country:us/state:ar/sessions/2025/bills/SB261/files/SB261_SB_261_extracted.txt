22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
23
24 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 27-21-102, concerning the definitions to be
25 used in relation to all-terrain vehicles, is amended to add an additional
26 subdivision to read as follows:
27 (5)(A) "Unpaved road" means a public street or highway that is
28 not paved with asphalt, chip-seal, concrete, brick, or stone.
29 (B) "Unpaved road" includes without limitation a road
30 constructed of:
31 (i) Dirt; or
32 (ii) Gravel.
33
34 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 27-21-106(a)(1), concerning the operation
35 of all-terrain vehicles on public streets and highways, is amended to read as
36 follows:
