25 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 21, Chapter 8, Subchapter 6, is amended
26 to add an additional section to read as follows:
27 21-8-608. Lobbying for covered foreign entity prohibited —— Definition
28 —— Penalties.
29 (a) As used in this section:
30 (1) "Covered foreign entity" means an individual, foreign
31 government, or a party other than an individual or foreign government:
32 (A) On the Consolidated Screening List or Entity List as
33 designated by the United States Secretary of Commerce;
34 (B) Domiciled in the People's Republic of China or the
35 Russian Federation;
36 (C) Under the influence or control by the government of
*SSS224*

1 the People's Republic of China or the Russian Federation; or
2 (D) That is a subsidiary of an individual, government, or
3 party referred to in subdivisions (a)(1)(A)-(C) of this section; and
4 (2) "Under the influence or control" means the People's Republic
5 of China, the Russian Federation, or an agent of the People's Republic of
6 China or the Russian Federation is:
7 (A) Exercising power or authority to regulate decisions
8 over the covered foreign entity; or
9 (B) Governing, managing, directing, or otherwise
10 overseeing decision making for the covered foreign entity.
11 (b) A person who receives a monetary benefit for lobbying shall not
12 lobby on behalf of a covered foreign entity.
13 (c) A person convicted of violating subsection (b) of this section
14 shall be guilty of:
15 (1) For the first offense, a violation with a fine of not less
16 than fifty dollars ($50.00) and not more than three thousand five hundred
17 dollars ($3,500);
18 (2) For the second offense, an unclassified misdemeanor with:
19 (A) A term of imprisonment not to exceed fix (6) months;
20 (B) A fine of five thousand dollars ($5,000); or
21 (C) Both the term of imprisonment and fine under
22 subdivisions (c)(2)(A) and (B) of this section; and
23 (3) For the third or subsequent offense, an unclassified felony
24 with:
25 (A) A term of imprisonment not to exceed one (1) year;
26 (B) A fine of ten thousand dollars ($10,000); or
27 (C) Both the term of imprisonment and fine under
28 subdivisions (c)(3)(A) and (B) of this section.
