28 SECTION 1. TITLE. This act shall be known and may be cited as the

3 (2) A constitutional amendment submitted to the people as herein

15 SECTION 3. Pursuant to the authority granted by Arkansas Constitution,
16 Article 5, § 1, the subsection of Arkansas Constitution, Article 5, § 1,
17 titled "Conflicting Measures", is amended to read as follows:
18 Conflicting Measures. If conflicting measures initiated or referred to
19 the people shall be approved by a majority of the votes severally cast for
20 and against the same as herein provided at the same election, the one
21 receiving the highest number of affirmative votes shall become law.
22
23 SECTION 4. EMERGENCY CLAUSE. It is found and determined by the
24 General Assembly of the State of Arkansas that amendments to the Arkansas
25 Constitution impact the public peace, health, and safety of each citizen of
26 this state; that to protect the public peace, health, and safety of the
27 citizens of this state, it is necessary to increase the threshold for passage
28 of a constitutional amendment proposed under Arkansas Constitution, Article
29 5, § 1, to ensure that constitutional amendments are supported by an
30 appropriate number of voters to justify an amendment to the Arkansas
31 Constitution; and that this act should become effective at the earliest
32 opportunity to ensure that its provisions are in place for the 2026 general
33 election. Therefore, an emergency is declared to exist, and this act being
34 immediately necessary for the preservation of the public peace, health, and
35 safety shall become effective on:
36 (1) The date of its approval by the Governor;
