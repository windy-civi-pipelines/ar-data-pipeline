26 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 26-57-604(a)(1)(B), concerning the credit
27 allowed against the premium tax based on the salary and wages of the
28 employees of the insurer, is amended to add an additional subdivision to read
29 as follows:
30 (iv) The credit shall not be applied as an offset
31 against the premium tax on collections reported as individual or group
32 comprehensive medical or hospital coverage.
33
