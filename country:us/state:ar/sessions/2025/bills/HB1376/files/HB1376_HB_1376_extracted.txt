16 TO ENCOURAGE STATE AGENCIES TO AWARD A
17 PORTION OF STATE CONTRACTS TO ARKANSAS
18 BUSINESSES THAT HAVE BEEN IN OPERATION
19 FOR LESS THAN FIVE YEARS.
20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
22
23 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 19, Chapter 11, Subchapter 1, is
24 amended to add an additional section to read as follows:
25 19-11-108. Award of contracts to Arkansas businesses in operation less
26 than five years — Definition.
27 (a) As used in this section, "state agency" means the same as defined
28 under § 19-11-203.
29 (b) The Office of State Procurement shall encourage each state agency
30 to award at least five percent (5%) of contracts entered into by the state
31 agency to businesses:
32 (1) That have been in operation for less than five (5) years;
33 and
34 (2) Whose principal place of business is in this state.
