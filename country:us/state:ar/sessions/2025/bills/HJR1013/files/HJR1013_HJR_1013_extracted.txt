18 A CONSTITUTIONAL AMENDMENT TO PROVIDE

22 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL

26 THAT the following is proposed as an amendment to the Constitution of
27 the State of Arkansas, and upon being submitted to the electors of the state
28 for approval or rejection at the next general election for Representatives

30 adopt the amendment, the amendment shall become a part of the Constitution of
31 the State of Arkansas, to wit:
32

34 read as follows:
35 § 1. Elective offices — Exceptions.

1 Congress of the United States, and in all elective state, district, circuit,
2 county, and township offices except those of Lieutenant Governor, Member of
3 the General Assembly and Representative in the Congress of the United States,
4 shall be filled by appointment by the Governor.
5
6 SECTION 2. EFFECTIVE DATE. This amendment to the Arkansas
7 Constitution is effective on and after January 1, 2027.
8
9 SECTION 3. BALLOT TITLE AND POPULAR NAME. When this proposed
10 amendment is submitted to the electors of this state on the general election
11 ballot:
12 (1) The title of this Joint Resolution shall be the ballot
13 title; and
14 (2) The popular name shall be "A Constitutional Amendment to
15 Provide that Vacancies in the Office of United States Senator, in the Office
16 of Representative in the Congress of the United States, and in All Elective
17 State, District, Circuit, and Township Offices Shall Be Filled by Appointment
18 by the Governor.".
