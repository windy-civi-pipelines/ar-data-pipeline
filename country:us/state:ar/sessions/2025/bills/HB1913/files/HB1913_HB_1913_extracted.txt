21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF ARKANSAS:
22
23 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code § 27-16-701(a)(2), concerning the application
24 for a driver's license or instruction permit, is amended to read as follows:
25 (2) The commercial driver's license or noncommercial driver's
26 license shall include the:
27 (A) An intermediate driver's license issued to persons who
28 are less than eighteen (18) years of age; and the
29 (B) A learner's license issued to persons who are less
30 than sixteen (16) years of age.
31
32 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code § 27-16-704(b)(7), concerning the examination
33 of an application for a driver's license, is amended to read as follows:
34 (7) In addition, the applicant for a learner's license and an
35 intermediate driver's license shall have the student's applicant's driving
36 record checked to verify that the student applicant has been free of a

18 age a restricted driver's license to be known as an “intermediate driver's

23 (ii) Any person sixteen (16) years of age or older

25 driving record is free of a serious accident and conviction of a serious
26 traffic violation for the most recent six-month period.

28 SECTION 6. Arkansas Code § 27-16-901(a)(1)(C), concerning the

32 (a) The driver's reaching eighteen (18) years
33 of age, and if:

35 free of a serious accident and conviction of a serious traffic violation for
