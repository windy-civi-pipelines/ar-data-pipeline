21 SECTION 1. Arkansas Code Title 21, Chapter 6, Subchapter 3, is amended
22 to add an additional section to read as follows:
23 21-6-312. Passport fees collected by county office.
24 (a) A county office that is a passport acceptance facility recognized
25 by the United States Department of State shall collect:
26 (1) The application and execution or acceptance fees for
27 processing passport applications; and
28 (2) Reasonable fees for passport photographs, mail services, and
29 expediting an application in accordance with and as permitted by federal law
30 or regulation as existing on January 1, 2025.
31 (b)(1) The fees collected under subsection (a) of this section that
32 are not directly remitted to the United States Department of State or the
33 United States Postal Service shall be paid into the county treasury to the
34 credit of the fund to be known as the "passport acceptance facility fund".
