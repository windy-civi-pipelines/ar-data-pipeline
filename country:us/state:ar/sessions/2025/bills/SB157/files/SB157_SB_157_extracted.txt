24 SECTION 1. DO NOT CODIFY. Legislative intent.
25 It is the intent of the General Assembly that:
26 (1) This act provide a pension-owned life insurance policy as a
27 benefit to the members of the public employee retirement systems;
28 (2) The pension-owned life insurance policies for the members of
29 the respective state retirement systems be owned by each respective system
30 and provided to each member of the system;
31 (3) Each state retirement system has the ability to offer life
32 insurance as a survivor's benefit to each member;
33 (4) No state retirement system shall be required to offer this
34 benefit; and
35 (5) If life insurance is offered as a survivor's benefit to the
36 members of a state retirement system, the life insurance payment shall be a

4 SECTION 2. Arkansas Code Title 24, Chapter 4, Subchapter 6, is amended

6 24-4-624. Life insurance — Payment to beneficiary.
