ARKANSAS SENATE
95th General Assembly - Regular Session, 2025
Amendment Form
Subtitle of Senate Bill 157
TO AMEND THE LAW CONCERNING BENEFITS UNDER THE ARKANSAS PUBLIC
EMPLOYEES' RETIREMENT SYSTEM.
______________________________________________________________________
Amendment No. 1 to Senate Bill 157
Amend Senate Bill 157 as originally introduced:
Delete Representative Warren as a cosponsor of the bill
AND
Add Representatives Perry, Warren as cosponsors of the bill
AND
Page 1, delete lines 10 and 11, and substitute the following:
"ARKANSAS PUBLIC EMPLOYEES' RETIREMENT
SYSTEM, STATE POLICE RETIREMENT SYSTEM,
ARKANSAS STATE HIGHWAY EMPLOYEES'
RETIREMENT SYSTEM, ARKANSAS TEACHER
RETIREMENT SYSTEM, AND ARKANSAS LOCAL
POLICE AND FIRE RETIREMENT SYSTEM; AND
FOR OTHER PURPOSES."
AND
Page 1, delete line 17, and substitute the following:
"STATE PUBLIC EMPLOYEE RETIREMENT
SYSTEMS."
AND
Delete everything after the enacting clause, and substitute the following:

Section 2:
SECTION 1. DO NOT CODIFY. Legislative intent.
It is the intent of the General Assembly that:
(1) This act provide a pension-owned life insurance policy as a
benefit to the members of the public employee retirement systems;
(2) The pension-owned life insurance policies for the members of
*.LJH269*
LJH269 - 03-27-2025 03:03:59 Page 1 of 3
the respective state retirement systems be owned by each respective system
and provided to each member of the system;
(3) Each state retirement system has the ability to offer life
insurance as a survivor's benefit to each member;
(4) No state retirement system shall be required to offer this
benefit; and
(5) Part of the life insurance payment be paid to the
beneficiary of a member and part of the life insurance payment be used to
lessen the system's unfunded liability, with the deceased member's
beneficiary receiving the net proceeds.

Section 3:
SECTION 2. Arkansas Code Title 24, Chapter 4, Subchapter 6, is amended
to add an additional section to read as follows:
24-4-624. Life insurance — Payment to beneficiary.
(a) The Arkansas Public Employees' Retirement System shall provide a
life insurance benefit to the beneficiary of a deceased member.
(b) The Board of Trustees of the Arkansas Public Employees' Retirement
System shall promulgate rules to implement this section.

Section 4:
SECTION 3. Arkansas Code § 24-5-116 is amended to add an additional
subsection to read as follows:
(e)(1) The Arkansas State Highway Employees' Retirement System shall
provide a life insurance benefit to the beneficiary of a deceased member.
(2) The Board of Trustees of the Arkansas State Highway
Employees' Retirement System shall promulgate rules to implement this
subdivision (e).

Section 5:
SECTION 4. Arkansas Code Title 24, Chapter 6, Subchapter 2, is amended
to add an additional section to read as follows:
24-6-229. Life insurance — Payment to beneficiary.
(a) The State Police Retirement System shall provide a life insurance
benefit to the beneficiary of a deceased member.
(b) The Board of Trustees of the State Police Retirement System shall
promulgate rules to implement this section.

Section 6:
SECTION 5. Arkansas Code Title 24, Chapter 7, Subchapter 7, is amended
to add an additional section to read as follows:
24-7-739. Life insurance — Payment to beneficiary.
(a) The Arkansas Teacher Retirement System shall provide a life
insurance benefit to the beneficiary of a deceased member.
(b) The Board of Trustees of the Arkansas Teacher Retirement System
shall promulgate rules to implement this section.

Section 7:
SECTION 6. Arkansas Code Title 24, Chapter 8, Subchapter 2, is amended
to add an additional section to read as follows:
24-8-231. Life insurance — Payment to beneficiary.
(a) The Arkansas Judicial Retirement System shall provide a life
insurance benefit to the beneficiary of a deceased member.
(b) The Board of Trustees of the Arkansas Judicial Retirement System
shall promulgate rules to implement this section.
