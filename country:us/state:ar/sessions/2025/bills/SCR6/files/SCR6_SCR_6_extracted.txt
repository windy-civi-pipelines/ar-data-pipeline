20 WHEREAS, the North Little Rock High School Charging Wildcats Varsity
21 Cheer Team won the Class 6A all-girl state championship held on December 20,
22 2024; and
23
24 WHEREAS, Charging Wildcats team members are Aspen Frensley, Avery
25 Bilon, Caitlin Jones, Ciriah Dunn, Iyuana Jones, Kamryn Cottrell, Kylie
26 Sartain, Leila Patton, Mallori Holloway, Nia Salaam, and Rikki Jimmerson, and
27 alternates Abby McCandless, Brooklyn Theodore, Evin Lawson, Jasiah Stith, and
28 Jenna Harrelson; and
29
30 WHEREAS, in additional to winning the state championship, the Charging
31 Wildcats attended four (4) competitions prior to the state championship and
32 placed first in three (3) of the four (4) competitions and placed third in
33 one (1) of the four (4) competitions; and
34

5 WHEREAS, the Charging Wildcats also traveled to Orlando, Florida, for
6 the National High School Cheerleading Championships in February 2025 and
7 placed eighth in a field of forty-three (43) teams; and
8
9 WHEREAS, Coach Missy Sela said of the win, "This team worked incredibly

11 cheerleaders on the mat, [which is] a first for a team to have that few
12 members win in the 6A division",
13
14 NOW THEREFORE,
15 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE SENATE OF THE NINETY-FIFTH GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE
16 STATE OF ARKANSAS, THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES CONCURRING THEREIN:
17
18 THAT the Senate, the House of Representatives concurring therein,
19 recognize the North Little Rock High School Charging Wildcats Varsity Cheer
20 Team for winning the Class 6A state championship.
21
22 BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED THAT upon adoption, a copy of this resolution be
23 presented to Coach Missy Sela by the Secretary of the Senate.
