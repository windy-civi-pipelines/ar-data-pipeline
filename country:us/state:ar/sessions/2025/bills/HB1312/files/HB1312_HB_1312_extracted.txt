21 SECTION 1. DO NOT CODIFY. Legislative findings and intent.
22 (a) The General Assembly finds that:
23 (1) It is the duty of the General Assembly to provide a suitable
24 and efficient free public education to the students of Arkansas;
25 (2) Acts 2023, No. 237, the "LEARNS Act", ensured that public
26 school teachers receive a minimum salary of fifty thousand dollars ($50,000)
27 and that public school teachers with salaries above fifty thousand dollars
28 ($50,000) receive a raise in the amount of two thousand dollars ($2,000);
29 (3) The House Committee on Education and the Senate Committee on
30 Education, meeting jointly, determined through the adequacy review process
31 the foundation funding amounts to meet the obligation of the General Assembly
32 to provide a suitable and efficient free public education to students; and
33 (4) The 2025-2026 school year foundation funding amount
34 recommended and adopted by the General Assembly includes three hundred twenty
35 dollars ($320) to pay the minimum employer contribution for participants in
36 the state-sponsored insurance program that public school districts pay and

3 (1) Provide funding to assist public school districts to meet
